An IT engineer has been charged with the murder of a man stabbed to death in Hendon, north-west London, last week.Terae Eusebe, 20, will appear in court next week accused of killing a 38-year-old Lithuanian national in the afternoon on Thursday.Two teenage girls aged 16 and 17 and a 19-year-old man were also arrested following the death.They have all been bailed until September pending further inquiries.Police and paramedics were called at 4.45pm on Thursday and found the man at the junction of Brent Street and Parson Street.He died at the scene just before 5.30pm. A postmortem examination on Friday found he died of stab wounds to the chest.Eusebe, of East Ham, will appear at Hendon magistrates court on Monday.
