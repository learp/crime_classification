Imran Asif Ali, 19, of Mitcham, and Quadeer Khan, 22, of Wandsworth, both from south London, appeared before magistrates in Clapham yesterday charged with the murder of brothers Mohammad Ali, 24, and Hayder Ali, 23, both from south London, who were stabbed last Saturday after a group of men surrounded them as they sat in their Mercedes van in Tooting.The accused were remanded in custody to appear at the Old Bailey on August 2. Thirteen people have been arrested over the murders. Eight are now in custody and five have been released on bail.
