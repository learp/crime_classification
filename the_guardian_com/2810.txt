A 42-year-old man was today charged with the rape and murder of pregnant teenager Amy Williams, police said.Amy, 14, was found dead in the grounds of St Michael's Church in Madeley, Telford, Shropshire, on December 27.A post-mortem examination found the teenager had been strangled.Amy, who was six months pregnant, disappeared after leaving a family party at about 9pm on Boxing Day.The suspect, whose identity has not been released, was the third man arrested in connection with the killing.He was arrested on Saturday and magistrates granted a warrant of detention yesterday afternoon, allowing officers to question the man for a further 36 hours.Two men, aged 28 and 27, have previously been questioned before being freed without charge.
