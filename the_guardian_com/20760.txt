A 24-year-old man has been remanded in custody accused of attempting to murder two teenage campers whose tent was hit by a van.Benjamin Allerton, of Millgate in Selby, North Yorkshire, spoke only to confirm his name, age and address during a 10-minute hearing at York crown court.He is accused of trying to kill a 16-year-old boy and 13-year-old girl who were camping in a field near Selby between the river Ouse and the road to York. Police were called to the scene near Barlby village at 3.55am on Sunday and found the boy seriously hurt and the girl with less severe injuries.Allerton will appear at York crown court on 19 March.
