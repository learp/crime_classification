A man was in a critical condition yesterday after being shot by armed men wearing Halloween masks.Police said the gang called at a house with "Happy Halloween" decorations in the window on Monday night. When a woman answered the door in Huyton, Merseyside, one of the men said "trick or treat". As another occupant, the man, 31, reached the door, one of the callers opened fire.The victim was taken to hospital with gunshot wounds. His condition is critical but stable. Detective Chief Inspector Phil Scarth said: "We believe the address was targeted." Police are looking for the men.
