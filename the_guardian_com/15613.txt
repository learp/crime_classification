A woman who believes she could have been attacked by serial killer Peter Tobin almost 40 years ago has spoken about her ordeal.Appearing on the BBC's Crimewatch programme tonight, the unidentified woman claimed Tobin put something in her drink before assaulting her in a Glasgow tenement in 1968.She contacted police after recognising a magazine photograph of the 63-year-old when he was younger. He would have been 21 at the time.Tobin is serving three life sentences for the murders of schoolgirl Vicky Hamilton, 15, Dinah McNicol, 18, both in 1991, and Angelika Kluk, 23, in 2006.
