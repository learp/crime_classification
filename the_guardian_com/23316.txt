Three men are to be charged with the murders of an elderly couple who were attacked with a hammer and a knife in a brutal attack at their home, police said.West Midlands police said officers had been given permission to charge Wojciech Ostolski, Lieneusz Bartnowski and Macaej Kus with the murders of Guiseppe and Caterina Massaro, who were found dead at their home in Wolverhampton last Friday.Ostolski, 32, Bartnowski, 21, and 30-year-old Kus, who are all understood to be from Poland, will appear before magistrates in Wolverhampton on Thursday to answer charges of murder and aggravated burglary, a police spokesman said.
