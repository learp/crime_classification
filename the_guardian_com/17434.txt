A man in his 20s was taken to hospital last night following a suspected drive-by shooting on Waterloo Bridge in the centre of London.His injuries were not life threatening, according to Scotland Yard, who said that three people were being questioned at various London police stations.The bridge's southbound carriageway was closed while detectives examined the scene after the Metropolitan Police were alerted at 8pm on Friday.A Metropolitan Police spokesman said: "A car is believed to have been shot at by someone on a motorcycle."A man in his 20s has been taken to hospital. His injuries are not thought to be life-threatening. Three people have been arrested in connection with the incident."The car came to a stop on the bridge, the force said.Members of the public said that they saw armed police at the scene and a number of people in handcuffs being taken to police vans.
