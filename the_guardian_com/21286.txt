Police arrested a woman on suspicion of murder today after the body of a four-year-old girl was found at the child's home.The body was discovered at a house in Wallasey, Merseyside. Police were called to the Oakdale Avenue house at 1.40am and arrested a 22-year-old woman.A postmortem examination will be carried out to confirm the cause of death.A Merseyside police statement said: "The [murder] investigation is in its early stages and crime scene investigators are at the address. House-to-house inquiries are also taking place."
