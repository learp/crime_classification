Five people have been charged with the kidnapping of a 10-year-old boy, who was rescued after a 36-hour ordeal. The boy was snatched from his home in Walthamstow, east London, on Thursday morning and was freed on Friday night from a flat on an estate in Victoria, in the city centre.Dawn Fields, 25, and Jeriah Simpson, 20, from Clapham, south London, face charges of kidnap, false imprisonment and blackmail. Three men from New York, Damien Francis, 24, Gadwell Gibbs, 23, and Edwin Phillip, 21, were also charged. All five are due to appear before Horseferry Road magistrates court today.
