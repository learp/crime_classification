The stabbing victim Abigail Witchalls, 26, has returned home from hospital.She was pushing her son Joseph in a buggy in a lane in Little Bookham, Surrey, on April 20 when she was knifed in the neck. The attack left her severely paralysed and she has spent the last five months at the Royal National Orthopaedic hospital in north London.The hospital said yesterday that she was now able to breathe and speak freely. She also had some movement in her right arm. It added: "It is expected she will soon need to be admitted to a maternity hospital as her pregnancy comes to term."
