A murder investigation has been launched following the death of a man who police and paramedics found with stab wounds in the early hours of the morning in the London suburb of East Ham.Three men and a woman were arrested at a residential address in east London on Monday morning on suspicion of murder.Scotland Yard said that officers were called by members of the public shortly after 1am to reports of a disturbance in Edwin Avenue. Police and ambulance staff found a man in his 30s who was pronounced dead at the scene.The Metropolitan police said that the force believed it knew the identity of the man but was waiting for a formal identification. A postmortem examination is scheduled to take place on Tuesday at Poplar Mortuary.Of those arrested, a man and a woman were bailed to return on a date in mid August while the other two men remained in custody at an east London police station.
