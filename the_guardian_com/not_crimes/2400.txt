In deepest winter, hot drinks need to up the ante, and this non-alcoholic punch does just that. It has enough of a spicy thwack to banish seasonal gloom or chills. Serves two to three.1 blade mace1 slice ginger (or 1 big pinch dried ginger flakes)6 allspice berries3 cloves½ quill cassia or cinnamon 500mls apple juice100mls water2-3tbsp honey, or to tastePut everything in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Turn down the heat and simmer for three to five minutes, strain and serve hot.• Claire Thomson, 5oclockapron.com
