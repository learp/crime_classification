This is our signature aperitif at Gymkhana restaurant in London. The citrus makes it a very refreshing palate-cleanser that helps take the edge off the working day. One is never enough, three perhaps too many. Serves one.60ml London dry gin8 dashes Angostura bitters 120ml tonic water (we use Fevertree)1 good-sized wedge each fresh lime, lemon and grapefruitPour the gin, bitters and tonic into a tall glass over lots of ice, squeeze in the juice from all the fruit and serve at once.• James Stevenson, group head bar manager, JKS Restaurants.
