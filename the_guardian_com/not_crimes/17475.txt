Facebook    Twitter    Pinterest   How will you be celebrating Harry Potter Book Night?The favourite spell from the Harry Potter books, with over 2700 votes, is:Expecto Patronum!One of the most famous and powerful spells, Expecto Patronum is important throughout the Harry Potter book series. It first appears in Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, taught to Harry Potter by Professor Lupin: The Patronus is a kind of positive force, a projection of the very things that the Dementor feeds upon – hope, happiness, the desire to survive – but it cannot feel despair, as real humans can, so the Dementors can’t hurt it. But I must warn you, Harry, that the Charm might be too advanced for you. Many qualified wizards have difficulty with it.”– Harry Potter and the Prisoner of AzkabanHarry Potter Night of Spells - quiz Accio and Wingardium Leviosa weren’t far behind, making second and third place as a result of your votes.
