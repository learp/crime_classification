The Federal Bureau of Investigation has said it is deciding whether to pursue and investigate an alleged incident regarding the actor Brad Pitt and his children while travelling on a private jet.Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie: a marriage that started – and ended – on screen The FBI told Variety on Thursday it was “continuing to gather facts” and would evaluate whether an investigation at the federal level would be pursued.The LA Times reported that the Bureau had been informed of “a child welfare incident” involving Pitt on an international flight last week by a law enforcement source who was not authorised to discuss the matter.
