Well, we all need a winter warmer on Bonfire night, don’t we? Serves one.25ml vodka (we use Wild Knight, an English brand made with Norfolk barley)25ml calvados½ tsp demerara sugar100ml apple juicePut everything in a small saucepan and warm gently, stirring, until the sugar dissolves. Serve in a rocks glass.• Paul Richardson, bar manager, Tuddenham Mill, Newmarket, Suffolk.
