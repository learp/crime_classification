I’ll tell you what I want, what I really, really want ... and that’s to see Guardian readers’ Spice Girls memorabilia?It’s 20 years since the release of the band’s first single Wannabe, a song that triggered the birth of girl power everywhere. To celebrate, we want to hear from those who collected – among other things – the Spice Girls photo album (remember that?) or got themselves a union jack dress. What memories do you have from the band? How did baby, ginger, sporty, posh and scary affect your life? What collectable items did you come across during your fandom? Were you part of a Spice Girls tribute band that performed during school break?
