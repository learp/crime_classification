He might have missed the traditional Christmas book rush, but Sinn Féin leader Gerry Adams is publishing a book of his best tweets.The party’s bookshop website says that the tweets by the “well known public figure” have been described as “bizarre, weird and as part of a clever strategy”. The tweets in the compact book have been “selected by the man himself”.
