Theatre-land favourite Joe Allen is 40 tomorrow, and this timeless classic from our list is as good a way as any to celebrate. It’s essentially an espresso martini, and easy to recreate at home. Serves one.25ml vodka (we use Stolichnaya)25ml shot espresso25ml Kahlúa2 coffee beans, to garnishPour the vodka and espresso over ice in a cocktail shaker, add the Kahlúa and shake hard. Strain into a cocktail glass and finish with a couple of coffee beans.• Stewart Moss, restaurant manager, Joe Allen, London WC2.
