Born in London, David Haye, 35, was world cruiserweight super champion before going on to become WBA heavyweight champion in 2009, defending his title twice. After three years out of the ring due to injury, “the Hayemaker” won his first comeback fight last January. His next fight, against Arnold “the Cobra” Gjergjaj, takes place at the O2 arena on 21 May.
