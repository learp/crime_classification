Plus, don’t forget to scroll all the way to the bottom for the chance to ask Veronica your questions!Carve the Mark is the title, and that’s pretty much all we know at this stage. It will be the first in a duology and Roth has said previously that it is a sci-fi fantasy set against a backdrop of political turmoil, telling the story of a boy named Akos who is kidnapped with his brother. To save his brother’s life, Akos must try to form an unlikely alliance with the dictator’s sister, Cyra, and dangerous mayhem – we are assured – will ensue.So now we have new clues!
