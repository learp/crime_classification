A much-loved view of St Paul’s Cathedral has been affected by a new development in east London.Conservationists have complained to the London mayor Sadiq Khan over the misleadingly named Manhattan Loft Gardens, a 42-floor tower in Stratford, which they say compromises the view of Wren’s masterpiece from King Henry’s Mound in Richmond Park.
