Born in Yorkshire, Blessed, 79, trained at Bristol Old Vic Theatre School. In 1980 he played Vultan in Flash Gordon, then Old Deuteronomy in Andrew Lloyd Webber’s musical Cats; he has appeared in RSC productions and four of Kenneth Branagh’s Shakespeare films. From 7 July he directs his wife, Hildegard Neil, and daughter, Rosalind Blessed, in Agatha Christie’s The Hollow at The Mill at Sonning, Reading.
