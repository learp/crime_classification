Actor Florence Henderson has died aged 82. Henderson, who had a long stage and screen career, and was most well-known as Carol Brady, the ever-cheerful mother in The Brady Bunch television show. Henderson died surrounded by family and friends, her manager, Kayla Pressman, said in a statement late Thursday.
