Britney Spears is fronting an ad campaign for EE to promote a tie-up with Apple Music.The mobile operator is offering new and upgrading customers six months’ access to Apple Music, the first time the service has partnered with a mobile operator in the UK.Spears will front a multimillion-pound ad campaign with the current face of EE, Kevin Bacon. EE chief executive Marc Allera said they had chosen Spears for a recognisable and retro feel to the ad campaign.
