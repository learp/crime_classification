The first glimpse of House of Cards’ fourth season has arrived, and while it’s only 25 seconds long, it’s rich with intrigue. Like his real-life, hopefully less ruthless counterparts, Frank Underwood, played by Kevin Spacey, is building his presidential campaign for 2016. It’s all smiles as his trusty chief of staff Doug Stamper drives a spade into some leafy sod, but tension flickers between them – perhaps because Stamper has already proved himself to be handy with a shovel...The season premieres on Friday 4 March, on Netflix.
