What better way to round off a disappointing year than to take part in the Guardian’s most disappointing New Year’s tradition: our GuardianWitness roundup of your NYE photos.We are not expecting glamour, celebration and excitement. In fact, we discourage it. We want your photos of terrible parties, blurry fireworks, and 3am kebabs. In short: we want your underwhelming photos.Wherever you are in the world, if you’re having a nondescript and average New Year’s Eve, share your images and videos with us via GuardianWitness.We will put together a gallery of the most disappointing tomorrow.Happy new year.You can share your experiences via GuardianWitness by clicking on the blue ‘Contribute’ button on this article.
