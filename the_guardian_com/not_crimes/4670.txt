This works as well in a jug to serve a crowd as in a glass for one; just multiply the quantities accordingly. It’s all but guaranteed to release your inner dancehall queen. Serves one.50ml Wray & Nephew overproofed Jamaican rum50ml orange juice50ml pineapple juice25ml grenadine15ml fresh lime juicePineapple wedge and mint, to garnishPut all the liquids in a shaker with some ice and hard shake. Pour into a hurricane or highball glass filled with fresh ice and garnish.• Ian Burrell, founder, RumFest, 22-23 October at ILEC in London.
