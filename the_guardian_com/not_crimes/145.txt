Complex, haunting, and profoundly literary, Didactic Poetries is French poet Phillipe Beck’s response to Schiller’s statement: “We are still waiting for a didactic poem where thought itself would be and would remain poetic.” In recognition of his entire oeuvre, Beck was awarded the French Academy’s Grand Prix de Poésie (Grand Poetry Prize) in 2015.
