The Minneapolis rapper, gospel singer and classically trained flautist Lizzo received praise for Big Grrrl Small World – an album that is filled with enlightenment and activism but, according to the Guardian’s Alexis Petridis: “never feels like a lecture. Quite the opposite: what makes it such a great album is how gleeful it all sounds.”Now she brings us her latest single Humanize, a futuristic blend of pop euphoria and hip-hop production which describes an internal struggle to accept intimacy.
