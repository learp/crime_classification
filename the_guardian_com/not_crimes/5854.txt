A rival to the classic toddy: the cloves and lemon bring spice and aroma, the seaweed adds salt. Raise a glass to apple growers. Serves one.50ml water2 cloves1 strip lemon peel1 thumb-sized piece dried kombu (or 1 pinch dried sea spaghetti seaweed) 2 tsp honey60ml cider brandy (I use Three-Year Old Somerset Cider Brandy)2 tsp lemon juiceGently heat water, cloves, peel and seaweed; before it simmers, take off heat and stir in honey. Put brandy in a warmed glass, top with strained infused water and add lemon juice.• Jack Adair Bevan is a drinks writer, co-founder of the Ethicurean in Bristol and co-creator of the Collector vermouth.
