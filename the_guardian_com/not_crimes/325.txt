Colin Farrell is set to reunite with The Lobster director Yorgos Lanthimos for dark new thriller The Killing of a Sacred Deer.According to Variety, Farrell, whose role as a single man forced to find love in last year’s dystopian dating satire won him many plaudits, will play a surgeon in what’s been described as a psychological thriller with supernatural elements.The mysterious plot has Farrell’s character somehow connected to a teenage boy and his family. When the boy’s behaviour turns sinister, he’s forced to make a sacrifice. The latter, a historical drama with Olivia Colman, Emma Stone and Rachel Weisz, centres on Queen Anne during the end of the 17th century.Farrell will next be seen in the adaptation of JK Rowling’s Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them alongside Eddie Redmayne, Samantha Morton and Katherine Waterston.
