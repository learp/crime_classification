Jamie Oliver has revealed the name of his new baby son – River Rocket Oliver.The celebrity chef posted a picture on Instagram of his fifth child snoozing in the garden as he formally introduced him to the world.Oliver said that River was snoozing on such a hot day, and that it was “probably polite to formally introduce the new boy to you”.The family had kept quiet about their son’s name since he arrived on 8 August.However, Oliver’s wife Jools recently dropped a big hint when she posted an Instagram message reading “Dream Big River” in capitals and added an emoji of a rocket and another of a heart.River Rocket joins siblings Poppy Honey, 14, Daisy Boo, 12, Petal Blossom, seven, and Buddy Bear, five.
