With the decline in the value of the pound and subsequent increase in the number of tourists coming to Britain (Report, 13 January), London galleries and museums should start charging them (Former MP backtracks on abolishing free entry, 14 January). A system whereby British taxpayers can enter for free while tourists pay, as in Italy, would certainly satisfy those who rarely get the opportunity to visit London to see what we pay for.
