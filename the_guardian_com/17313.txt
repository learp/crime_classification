Police chiefs paid tribute to a rookie woman police officer last night who was found stabbed to death with a man believed to be her partner.The 28-year-old policewoman was found dead at her home in Oakham, Rutland, last night. Post-mortem examinations found that both had died of stab wounds. Police said they were not looking for anyone else in connection with the deaths.The policewoman, who has not yet been named, was a probationary officer working on the Leicester city beat with 18 months' service.She was a hard-working and popular officer who will be sadly missed, Chief Superintendent Chris Rollings said.'The death of a serving officer is clearly an extremely tragic event,' he added
