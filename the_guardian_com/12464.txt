A man was today charged with the murder of Finnish student Suvi Aronen, whose body was found in undergrowth in Wanstead, east London, last week. Miss Aronen, 23, a student at the University of East London, was attacked late on Thursday afternoon and her body found the next day. Unemployed Jason Dockrill, 33, was due to appear before magistrates at Redbridge later today, Scotland Yard said. Mr Dockrill, of West Road, Stratford, was also charged with three further offences said to have taken place in Wanstead on Thursday. He has been charged with the assault and attempted robbery of a 44-year-old woman, assaulting an 80-year-old woman and robbing a 56-year-old woman.
