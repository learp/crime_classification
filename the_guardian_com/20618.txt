Police have arrested a man in connection with an attack on a promising musician by a fellow bus passenger.The man walked into a London police station to talk to officers about the attack on Billy Leeson in Islington, north London. A Metropolitan police spokesman said the man was in custody, and police made an appeal for witnesses to come forward.Leeson, who plays with the band Les Incompetents, became involved in a fight after a row about "unwanted attention" which his girlfriend was receiving. He suffered head injuries and was in a critical but stable condition in hospital.
