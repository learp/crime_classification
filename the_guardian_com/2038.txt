A teenager has been charged with murdering a songwriter, police have said.Jack Hummerstone is accused of the murder of Thomas Brittain in a flat in Colchester, Essex, in March. He will appear on Monday at Chelmsford magistrates court.The 18-year-old, of no fixed address, was arrested in Islington, north London, at 3am on Sunday.He has also been charged with grevious bodily harm with intent and possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life.Brittain, 26, from Essex, died after being stabbed in the early hours of 9 March.He was a songwriter and local music producer also known as Twister. His family described him as "a lovely, kind and funny man who will be dearly missed," at the time of his killing.A 22-year-old man from London who was arrested on suspicion of assisting an offender has been released on police bail pending further inquiries until 30 May.
