Police have arrested a man wanted in connection with the murder of a woman in a flat in west London.Robert Richard Fraser was arrested on Tuesday night in the Leicester Square area and has been taken to a central London police station, Scotland Yard said.The body of Maria Duque-Tunjano, a 48-year-old Briton originally from Colombia, who was working as a prostitute, was discovered at a flat in Earls Court, west London, on Friday. Detectives had linked her murder to an attack on another sex worker on 18 January."Police were appealing for assistance in locating Robert Richard Fraser following the murder of Maria Duque-Tunjano on Friday," a Scotland Yard statement said. "We thank the media and the public for their assistance with this appeal."
