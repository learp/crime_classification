A boy, 14, from Bury, Greater Manchester, will appear before magistrates today accused of murdering Joe Geeling, 11, who was killed on Wednesday while walking home from school.His parents, Tom and Gwen, yesterday made an emotional statement after friends and relatives had gathered at church."Everybody wants us to give a few comments on how much Joe meant to us and what a loss he will be," they said. "None of you have enough time to hear how much he meant to us. How do you condense 11 years of fun and love and heartache, worries and accomplishments etc into a few words?"
