A 14-year-old boy appeared in court yesterday charged with the murder of 11-year-old Joe Geeling, a cystic fibrosis sufferer.Joe, a Manchester City fan from Bury, Greater Manchester, was found stabbed to death in Whitehead Park, Ainsworth, Bury, on March 2.The teenager, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was remanded in secure accommodation until March 13 at Bury youth court. He spoke only to confirm his name and address as the charge was put to him during a 20-minute hearing.No plea was entered and no application for bail was made.
