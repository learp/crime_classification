A man has been charged with raping an 11-year-old girl. The 31-year-old from Cinderford in the Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire, is charged with two counts of rape, both said to have taken place last year.The girl cannot be named for legal reasons. Gloucestershire police spokeswoman Zoe Young said: "We can confirm that a 31-year-old male was arrested on May 10 and charged with two counts of rape."He appeared before Cheltenham magistrates court on Saturday. The case was adjourned to Gloucester crown court for hearing on May 24. The man was remanded in custody.
