A female teacher has admitted having sex with two 15-year-old male pupils at a Merseyside school.Hina Patel, who worked as a supply teacher at Birkdale High school, near Southport, met the pupils at her home, where they had sexual intercourse, North Sefton magistrates were told.Patel, of Sandilands Grove, Hightown, pleaded guilty yesterday to two charges of abusing her position of trust through sexual misconduct with the boys, who cannot be identified because of their age.The 37-year-old was suspended when the allegations emerged earlier this year.The chairman of the bench, Caroline Spencer, bailed Patel on condition that she did not contact the boys or any pupils at the school.She will be sentenced at Liverpool crown court on 22 November.
