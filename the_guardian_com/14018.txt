Police were questioning 12 people on suspicion of murder after a man in his 30s was stabbed to death at a house in Littlehampton, West Sussex, on Christmas Day.Police were called to the address around 9pm, where the man was found bleeding heavily from a stab wound; ambulance staff pronounced him dead at the scene."A number of people have been arrested on suspicion of murder after a man was stabbed in Littlehampton," said a police spokesman.
