Three men appeared in court yesterday charged with the manslaughter of the youngest Briton to reach the summit of Mount Everest more than six years ago.Jonathan Tinker, Henry Todd and Michael Smith appeared before York magistrates charged with unlawfully killing Michael Matthews, 22, between May 12 and 15 1999.Mr Matthews has been presumed dead since he disappeared as he descended the peak in a 100mph blizzard. District judge Martin Walker, adjourning the case, ordered the defendants to appear at the Central criminal court in London on June 6.
