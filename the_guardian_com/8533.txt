A 41-year-old woman was stabbed to death at her home in Wrexham, north Wales, after her former partner forced his way into the property at the weekend and attacked her with a knife.Tracy Jones died and her brother, who was not named, was injured in the attack. Michael Andrew Evans, the chief suspect, was later found hanged in a garage in the nearby village of Llay. Police are not looking for anyone else in connection with the killing. Separately, a man, 41, has been charged with the murder of Claire Umali, 39 and her son Kim, 13 in Chester after they died following an incident at their home.
