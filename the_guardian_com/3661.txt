Birmingham police launched a murder investigation at the weekend when a five-year-old girl died in hospital after suffering severe burns in an arson attack on her home.The girl, who received 95% burns after becoming trapped inside the house, died on Saturday evening.Five other members of her family, thought to be of Bangladeshi origin, were released from hospital several hours after their home in Perry Barr was attacked.Police said two Asian men in dark clothing had been seen entering the house.Detectives said they did not know the motive behind the attack.
