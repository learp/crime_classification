Detectives are looking for two black men seen running from the scene of a murder in south London on Friday.The men were seen running away from Coldharbour Lane, Camberwell, where a 26-year-old man, who had been stabbed then shot dead in a vicious attack, had been found. The victim was pronounced dead at Kings College Hospital shortly after the shooting at 1pm on Friday. It is believed that the victim had been involved in a dispute, which had began inside a flat near the junction with Denmark Hill. Police believe the victim had been attacked at the address before staggering into Coldharbour Lane where he collapsed. Detectives from Trident, Scotland Yard's gang unit, are appealing for witnesses.
