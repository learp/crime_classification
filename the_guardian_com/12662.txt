A 31-year-old man appeared in court yesterday accused of murdering seven-year-old Toni-Ann Byfield.Joel Smith, of no fixed address, appeared before magistrates in London also charged with the murder of 41-year-old Anthony Pinnock. Mr Pinnock, also known as Tony Byfield and Bertram Byfield, was believed at the time to be Toni-Ann's father. Smith was remanded in custody to appear before the Old Bailey on January 23.Mr Pinnock and Toni-Ann were shot dead in his bedsit in Harlesden, north-west London, in September 2003. Toni-Ann was in the care of social services.
