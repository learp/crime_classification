A man has been stabbed to death and another seriously injured in a machete attack in Manchester city centre.The man, in his 20s, of Chinese origin, was found in an alley with serious stab wounds to his groin. He was taken to hospital after the attack on Monday night but died early yesterday. Another man, aged 24, received serious wounds to his arms, legs and head but his injuries are not thought to be life threatening. A police statement said: "Detectives were called to Princess Street following reports of a man with a bladed instrument."
