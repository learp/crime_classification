A teenage boy has been stabbed to death in south London in a suspected gang-related killing. Police were called to a block of flats in Southwark early yesterday after reports that a youth had been stabbed on a stairwell.The victim, 15, from Peckham, was confirmed dead at the scene. Four teenagers, aged between 15 and 17, were being questioned by police last night. This year, 12 teenagers have been killed in the capital.A separate murder inquiry is under way after Michael Mann, believed to have been in his forties, was stabbed to death on Willesden High Road, north-west London, just after 5pm on Friday. An 18-year-old man, a woman and a man in his forties were yesterday helping police with their inquiries.
