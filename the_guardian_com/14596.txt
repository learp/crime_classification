A second woman arrested in connection with a child abuse inquiry centred on a Plymouth nursery was remanded in custody yesterday after being charged with sex offences.Angela Allen, 39, appeared in court following her arrest in Nottingham as part of a police investigation at Little Ted's nursery. She faces a total of 16 charges alleged to have been committed between April 2006 and 17 June this year, including four offences of sexual assault on a girl aged under 13. She was detained by Nottinghamshire police as part of the same inquiry that led to the arrest of Plymouth nursery worker Vanessa George earlier this month. George, 39, has been charged with sexual assault and making and distributing indecent images of children.
