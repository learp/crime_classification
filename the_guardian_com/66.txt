A teenager has been detained by detectives investigating the rape of an 11-year-old girl who was attacked at a supermarket, police said yesterday. Warwickshire police said the 15-year-old boy was arrested at an address in Leamington Spa on Wednesday night.The victim, whose mother made an anonymous plea for information, was assaulted in a toilet cubicle on March 2 at the Sainsbury's store at a retail park in Leamington Spa. A police spokesman said the 15-year-old was in custody awaiting questioning. The victim is said to have been left traumatised by the attack.
