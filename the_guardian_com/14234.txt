Burglars were caught red-handed when John Ellison, 52, was alerted to intruders in his home by an automatic call to his mobile phone while on holiday in Malaga, Spain.Using his laptop, he logged on to his CCTV system and called the police after seeing the men in his home in Lancaster. "I saw three men hurrying through the house doing damage," said the company director. "Then I saw the police go upstairs and saw them come down with two men in handcuffs." Two men who admitted burglary at Lancaster magistrates court will be sentenced on October 5.
