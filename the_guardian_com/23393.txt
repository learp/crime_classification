A man has been bailed after allegedly threatening to kill the home secretary, Theresa May, and one of her predecessors, Jack Straw, police say.Detectives said that on Monday they arrested a 33-year-old man from Blackburn on suspicion of making threats to kill and malicious communication.The man has since been released on police bail pending further inquiries.The threats were allegedly made during a telephone call to the office of the Rossendale and Darwen MP, Jake Berry, on 18 April.
