Three men have been arrested on suspicion of murder after a man was discovered face down in a brook. The unidentified victim's body was found in Ribbleton, Preston, Lancashire, at 8.15am today by a member of the public. Underwater search teams scoured the brook, which is on common land between the Moor Nook and Grange estates. A Lancashire police spokesman said a postmortem examination would be carried out to establish the cause of death. Three men, aged 19, 21 and 26, from Ribbleton have been arrested on suspicion of murder and are in police custody.
