A 59-year-old man has been charged with the murder of a teenage girl who has not been seen since 2007.Paige Chivers, 15, left her home in Bispham, Blackpool in August 2007.Since then there have been no corroborated sightings of her and no contact with her family or friends, despite numerous appeals, rewards and a long-running police investigation.On Thursday, Lancashire police charged Robert Ewing, from Blackpool, with her murder.Ewing is also accused of intending to pervert the course of justice.A second man, Gareth Dewhurst, 45, from Blackpool, is charged with assisting an offender, a sexual offence and intending to pervert the course of justice, said police.A spokesman for Lancashire Constabulary said: “Following consultation with the Crown Prosecution Service two men are due to appear in court this morning in connection with the murder of Paige Chivers.“A 29-year-old man from Blackpool arrested on suspicion of attempting to pervert the course of justice has been released on police bail pending further inquiries.”Both accused are due to appear at Blackpool magistrates’ court later.
