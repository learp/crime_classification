A woman who had sex almost 200 times with a 12-year-old boy is facing a lengthy prison sentence.Angela Sullivan, 36, gave the boy a pair of trainers as a reward after he slept with her for the 100th time.When police arrested her in October, they found her diary marked with childish entries, and 191 stars against dates when they had sex.The unemployed single mother, of Cavendish Road, Middlesbrough, admitted 10 specimen charges at Teesside crown court and was remanded in custody. She will be sentenced next month.Judge Tony Briggs adjourned the case for psychiatric reports.
