Five people were being questioned last night about the discovery of the body of a missing woman who could have been murdered in an honour killing.The body of Banaz Mahmod Babakir Agha, 20, of Mitcham, south London, was found in a suitcase buried in the garden of a derelict house in Birmingham more than three months after her disappearance on January 23. Police believe the murder may have been linked to a failed arranged marriage. A postmortem examination is being carried out. A 29-year-old man has already been charged with murder in connection with the investigation.
