A woman "had her head kicked in" in a pub toilet by the wife of a champion jockey, magistrates in Suffolk were told today.Emma Witt, a 35-year-old claims advisor, told the court in Bury St Edmunds that Julie Fallon, the wife of the flat racing star Kieren Fallon, forced her to the floor and punched and kicked her.Fallon, 41, denies assaulting Ms Witt in the Star pub at Lidgate, also in Suffolk, in January.
