A woman will appear in court today charged with the murder of an 11-month-old baby boy.Mapaseka Justinah Shapung, 30, will appear at North Tyneside magistrates' court charged with killing the child, who has yet to be formally identified.His body was found in a flat in George Stephenson Way on the Royal Quays estate in North Shields, North Tyneside, on New Year's Day.Shapung was arrested in connection with the baby's death on Monday, and was charged last night following questioning.Detectives had been waiting for the result of further tests after a postmortem examination failed to establish a cause of death.A Northumbria Police spokesman said: "A 30-year-old woman, Mapaseka Justinah Shapung, has now been charged with murder in relation to the death of an 11-month-old boy in North Tyneside on New Year's Day."
