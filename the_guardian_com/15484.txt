Detectives in Greater Manchester questioned a teenager yesterday in connection with a series of sex attacks in Manchester and nearby townsPolice are linking the 14 assaults, including a rape, which have been carried out in Manchester, Stockport and Tameside since July. The arrest of the 17-year-old came after an announcement earlier this week that detectives were hunting a man who who rode a bike before confronting his victims. A team of detectives was set up to investigate the crimes, the first of which was committed on July 18 with the most recent on September 29. The attacks have generally occurred at weekends. Police said the attacker had approached victims on his black, red and silver mountain bike and confronted them verbally while producing or implying he had a knife.
