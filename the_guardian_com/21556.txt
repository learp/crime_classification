A policeman is being treated in hospital after being stabbed when he and a colleaugue stopped two men in west London today.The 22-year-old officer was attacked near White City. Scotland Yard said the second policeman was sprayed with a "noxious substance" during the incident. A Metropolitan police spokesman said the stabbed officer was being treated at a central London hospital, and was in a stable condition.The attacks happened in Bramley Road, close to the BBC Television Centre, at around 2.25pm. One suspect was arrested at the scene. The other suspect ran off, but was later arrested.
