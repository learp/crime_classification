A taxi driver has admitted murdering Sian O'Callaghan, who disappeared after a night out with friends last year.Christopher Halliwell, 48, of Swindon, pleaded guilty to the charge during a hearing at Bristol crown court.O'Callaghan, 22, left Swindon's Suju nightclub in the early hours of 19 March last year, and walked past the Goddard Arms on the high street before getting into Halliwell's taxi.Hundreds of volunteers turned out at Savernake Forest to help in the search for her. Police found her body in Uffington, Oxfordshire, on 24 March.
