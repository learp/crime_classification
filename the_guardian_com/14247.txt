A murdered teenager whose body was found in a suitcase died of asphyxiation, police said yesterday. Last night detectives continued to question a local man in connection with her death.Terry Edmonds, 17, disappeared on the way to meet her boyfriend, 26-year-old Nigel Fitzgerald, on Easter Monday in Tunbridge Wells, Kent. The body of the student, who said she wanted to be a nurse, was found on Saturday in a supermarket car park. Police are making house-to-house inquiries in the residential area surrounding the car park where her body was found.
