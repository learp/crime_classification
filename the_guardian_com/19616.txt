Detectives have begun a murder investigation after the discovery of a woman's body in woodland.Officers from Surrey police were called shortly before 11.30pm on Sunday after a call from a member of the public who was concerned about the woman's safety. They then attended an address in West Street, Haslemere.Following inquiries, they went to Blackdown Woods near Lurgashall, West Sussex, where a search of the woodland led to the discovery of the body of a woman in her 30s.Police have cordoned off an area of woodland while forensic officers carry out their investigations.The woman has not yet been formally identified and a postmortem is expected to take place on Tuesday at the Royal Surrey County hospital in Guildford, police said.A 35-year-old man from Haslemere has been arrested on suspicion of murder and remains in police custody.Anyone with information is asked to contact Surrey police on 01483 571212.
