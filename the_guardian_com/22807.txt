A 36-year-old man appeared in court yesterday charged with the murder of his wife and their three children. Rahan Arshad, who was arrested on Friday at Heathrow when he returned from Bangkok, appeared before magistrates in Stockport, Greater Manchester.He did not enter a plea and was remanded in custody. The bodies of Usma Rahan, 32, her sons Adam, 11, and Abbas, eight, and her daughter Henna, six, were found at the family home in Cheadle Hulme on August 20.In a statement read after the hearing Mr Arshad appealed to the media to respect the privacy of his family.
