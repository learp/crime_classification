Police have arrested a 17-year-old boy after the murder of a woman stabbed in a south London street.Carmelita Tulloch, 51, was attacked in a street in Kennington as she walked to work on Tuesday. A witness told police there had been a struggle and Mrs Tulloch had fallen to the ground with her attacker on top of her, stabbing her in a "frenzied manner". The mother of two, who had one granddaughter, died at the scene. Her assailant fled, leaving her handbag untouched.Friends described Mrs Tulloch, who worked as a receptionist and a cleaner, as a "lovely, hard-working woman".
