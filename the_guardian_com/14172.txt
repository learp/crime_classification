A taxi driver has denied murdering 22-year-old Sian O'Callaghan, who went missing after a night out with friends. Christopher Halliwell, 48, pleaded not guilty to the charge during a plea and case management hearing.O'Callaghan disappeared after leaving the Suju nightclub in Swindon, Wiltshire, in the early hours of 19 March last year. Less than a week later, police found O'Callaghan's body on the side of a road in Uffington, Oxfordshire.A trial, scheduled to last two weeks, is due to begin on 25 February next year.
