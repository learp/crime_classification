Police have been hunting rioters and looters by displaying CCTV footage of their crimes on a city centre mobile screen.The "digi-van", which has a 6m square display unit, was being used to parade pictures of suspects in the hope that Birmingham shoppers would recognise them.More than 50 CCTV images were being shown on the screen. Acting Detective Inspector Mark Rushton of West Midlands police said: "Photographs of those wanted by us have appeared in the media but we are determined to use every means available to us to bring suspected offenders to justice."
