Video clips of violent street fighting that killed two Leeds United fans were finally shown yesterday at the opening of an inquest in Leeds delayed for almost four years by criminal proceedings.Christopher Loftus, 35, a telecoms engineer, and Kevin Speight, 40, a Leeds pub landlord, were stabbed to death after becoming caught up in running battles in Istanbul before Leeds match against Galatasary in April 2000, West Yorkshire coroner David Hinchlif heard. Neither man had any record of violent behaviour. A 22-year-old Turkish man was jailed in May 2002 for killing them. Last year a Turkish appeals court overturned his sentence and ordered a retrial. The inquest continues.
