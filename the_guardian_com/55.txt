A man has been charged with attempted murder after another man was shot as he answered his front door.The victim, aged in his 30s, was taken by ambulance to the Royal Berkshire hospital in Reading for treatment to his injuries after the incident in Easthampstead, Bracknell, on 10 May.Scott Kennedy, 28, of Frimley, Surrey, has been charged with one count of attempted murder and he will appear at Reading Magistrates court later.
