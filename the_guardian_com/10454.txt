Police today confirmed that a body found floating in the sea was that of the wife of murdered businessman Amarjit Chohan.The body of 25-year-old Nancy Chohan was discovered floating in the water at Poole bay, Dorset, the Metropolitan police said. A spokeswoman said today: "Police can confirm the body of a female recovered from Poole Bay, Dorset, on July 15 is that of Nancy Chohan, aged 25 - Amarjit Chohan's wife. "The Met police have been liaising with Dorset police. Her death is being treated as murder and is being investigated by the specialist crime directorate west under DCI Norman Mackinlay. Next of kin has been informed."
