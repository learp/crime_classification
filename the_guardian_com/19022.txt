Police have launched a murder investigation after a teenage boy was stabbed to death.Officers were called in the early hours of Tuesday to reports that a 17-year-old had been attacked in Palmers Green, north London.Emergency services were called out at 00.45 but the teenager was pronounced dead at 01:26, Scotland Yard said.Police, the London Ambulance Service and the capital's Air Ambulance helicopter attended the scene in Bowes Road, a force spokesman said.He said the teenager's family had been informed.Formal identification had yet to take place and a postmortem examination would be held in due course, the spokesman added.There have been no arrests so far and police are appealing for anyone with information to call 020 8785 8244 or Crimestoppers, anonymously, on 0800 555 111.
