A police sergeant who tried to kill his ex-wife by sawing through her neck after he rammed her car was yesterday jailed for 12 years.John Kelly, 45, ploughed his vehicle head-on into Audrey McDowall's car a month after their divorce last year. Kelly, from Dumfries, then grabbed a saw from his Vauxhall Cavalier and jumped into his ex-wife's car, where he attacked her.He pleaded guilty to attempted murder at the high court last month. The court heard the couple had divorced in August 2005 but Kelly had not accepted that Ms McDowall had a new man in her life.
