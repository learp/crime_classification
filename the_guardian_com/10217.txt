A woman died from a stab wound and four children were hurt in a domestic incident in Huddersfield on Sunday. A man also suffered serious injuries.Officers were called to a home in Reinwood Road at 9.32pm following calls from the public, and arrested a man.They found a 37-year-old woman with a serious stab wound. She was taken to hospital but died.A nine-year-old boy was found outside the property with a stab wound to his arm. He was treated in hospital.West Yorkshire police said another nine-year-old boy, an 11-year-old boy and a six-month-old baby girl were also present and treated for minor injuries.DCI Ady Taylor said: "A 39-year-old man was detained at the scene and is being treated in hospital today for serious injuries."We are not looking for anyone else in connection with this incident."Enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and officers are patrolling the area, offering support and reassurance to local residents."He asked anyone with any information to contact the police on the non-emergency number, 101, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
