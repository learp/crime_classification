A British man has died after being attacked near his hotel in Portugal. Ian Haggath, 50, from Dunston near Gateshead, was beaten up in Albufeira two weeks ago and died from his injuries last Wednesday.A Foreign Office spokeswoman said: "I can confirm the death of a British national in Faro on 25 May. We are in touch with the family and we are providing consular assistance."According to reports, Mr Haggath suffered serious head injuries after being attacked by four people. There is speculation that the same gang are responsible for attacking two other tourists this year.
