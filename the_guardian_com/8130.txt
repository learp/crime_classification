A British man accused of shooting his American wife and baby daughter used the internet to research ways of killing people and how to commit suicide days before their murders, a court was told yesterday.Neil Entwistle, 27, originally from Worksop, Nottinghamshire, made his second appearance in court in the US to plead not guilty to the murders of his wife, Rachel, and nine-month-old daughter, Lillian.Prosecutors say Mr Entwistle shot them both in their home in Boston after racking up tens of thousands of dollars in debts and becoming dissatisfied with his sex life.
