A British man accused of murdering his American wife and baby daughter at their home in Hopkinton, Massachusetts, has been extradited to the United States, Scotland Yard said.Neil Entwistle, 27, was transferred into the custody of US marshals at Gatwick airport yesterday. A police spokesman said he understood that Mr Entwistle was returning to the US on a private flight.Mr Entwistle agreed to be extradited during a hearing at Bow Street magistrates' court last Friday. It is understood that he will be arraigned before a US court later this week.
