A man has been charged with possession of a submachine gun as part of an investigation by counter-terrorism detectives.Mohamed Elyasse Taleouine, 20, from Edgware, north London, is due to appear at Westminster magistrates court. He is charged with possession of a Mac 10-style submachine gun and 24 live 9mm cartridges.Taleouine was arrested on 10 January by officers investigating alleged support for travel overseas for terrorism. He has not been charged with any terrorism offences.He was held with another man, 37, outside a business address in west London. Searches were carried out at the business and at three residential addresses in west London. The second man was released without charge on Wednesday.
