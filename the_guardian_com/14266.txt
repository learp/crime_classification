Three men wanted in connection with the murder of the Glasgow teenager Kriss Donald appeared in court yesterday.The men, who cannot be named, were flown back to Glasgow from a foreign country on Wednesday to face trial.Kriss, 15, was murdered in the ethnically diverse Pollokshields area of the city in March last year.The teenager had been stabbed and set on fire.In December, Daanish Zahid, 20, was sentenced to life for his murder. Zahid Mohammed, 20, was cleared of murder but sentenced to five years for abduction and racially aggravated assault.
