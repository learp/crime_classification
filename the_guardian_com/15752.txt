A man has appeared in court accused of shooting dead his wife in north Devon on Sunday.David Leeman is charged with the murder of his wife, Jennie, at their farm at Parracombe, near Barnstaple.Leeman appeared before magistrates in Barnstaple, speaking only to confirm his personal details.Two of the couple's young sons attended the hearing, with the youngest breaking down in tears as his 59-year-old father appeared in the secure dock, flanked by security guards.Magistrates remanded Leeman in custody ahead of a bail hearing at Exeter crown court on Friday.He, his wife and their four children had lived on the farm, on the edge of a heavily wooded valley, for more than 10 years.The couple ran family companies Leeman UK Ltd and Leeman Holdings UK Ltd.
