A 10-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of shooting another boy, aged eight in South Tyneside, police said today.The injured boy was being treated at South Tyneside hospital and was in a serious condition, Northumbria police said.The shooting is thought to have taken place on a travellers' site at West Pastures in Boldon.Police said the case was reported by hospital staff at about 1pm after the victim was admitted for treatment.Superintendent Jim Napier said: "This incident is in its very early stages.
