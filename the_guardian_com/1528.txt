A man has been taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries following a shooting in Clapham, south London.Police were called to the incident on Clapham High Street, near the junction with Cato Road, at about 9.40pm on Wednesday night.A Metropolitan police spokesman said the victim, in his 20s, had been taken to a hospital in south London.He said: "The shooting was non-fatal but his injuries were described as life threatening."
