A British first officer serving on a millionaire's luxury yacht was raped while the boat was undergoing repairs in an Italian harbour.Police have not the named the 27-year-old woman or the 100ft yacht for legal reasons. The woman, from Merseyside, was attacked after she went ashore with fellow crew members for a drink while the yacht was being repaired in Genoa harbour.The crew went to the Rosa dei Venti, a popular bar with yacht hands. In her statement to police, the first officer said: 'After I bought some cigarettes I returned to the bar to join my friends but went to the public toilet first. As I tried to close the door this maniac blocked it. He must have been watching me and followed me.'
