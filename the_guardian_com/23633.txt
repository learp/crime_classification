A man and a woman were arrested in connection with the unexplained death of a baby yesterday. Police said the infant was admitted with injuries to the Horton General hospital in Banbury, Oxfordshire, on 5 November. The child was pronounced dead at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford last night. Detective Chief Inspector Joe Kidman, leading the investigation for Thames Valley police, said: "We are in the early stages of a detailed investigation to establish how this child died." A spokesman said that a man and a woman were arrested over the incident and were bailed pending further inquiries.
