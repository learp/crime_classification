A science teacher was today charged with the attempted murder of one of his students, police said.Peter Harvey, 49, is accused of attacking Jack Waterhouse, 14, at All Saints' Roman Catholic School in Mansfield on Wednesday morning.Harvey will appear before the town's magistrates tomorrow morning, Nottinghamshire police said.Jack was found by paramedics unconscious at the entrance to a classroom in the school's science block at 11am on Wednesday.He is currently in a stable condition at the Queen's Medical Centre in Nottingham, where he is being treated for serious head injuries.
