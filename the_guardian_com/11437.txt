A Premier League footballer has been arrested on suspicion of conspiracy to rob, it was revealed today.Aston Villa midfielder Isaiah Osbourne, 21, from Birmingham, was arrested at the club's training ground in Bodymoor Heath, north Warwickshire, on Friday.He has been bailed while further inquiries are carried out.A West Midlands police spokeswoman said: "A 21-year-old from Birmingham was arrested at the Villa training ground on October 2 on suspicion of conspiracy to rob."A spokeswoman for Aston Villa said the club was aware of the arrest but declined to comment further.Osbourne has also played on loan for Nottingham Forest and is a former England Under-16 international.
