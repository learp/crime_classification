A teenager appeared in court yesterday charged with stabbing three girls as they got off a bus in a Dorset town.Ian Andrew Ruby, 18, was arrested at Chertsey, Surrey, on Saturday, three days after the daylight attack in Bridport. He appeared at Bournemouth magistrates court charged with three offences of wounding with intent and possessing a machete and knife.The girls, aged 14, 15 and 17, were recovering from puncture wounds in Dorset county hospital in Dorchester. Mr Ruby, of Bridport, was remanded in custody and will appear at Dorchester crown court next Monday.
