A murder investigation has been launched after a woman was found dead at hotel in Bournemouth.A man has been arrested.Dorset police said they were alerted to reports of a serious incident at a hotel in St Michael's Road, where they discovered the body of a woman who has not yet been identified.Detective Inspector Stewart Balmer of the major crime investigation team said: "A murder investigation has been launched and is in its early stages."If anyone has any information about this incident I urge them to call Dorset police at the earliest possible opportunity."Anyone with any information should call Dorset police in confidence on 101.
