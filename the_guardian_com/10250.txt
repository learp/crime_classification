Detectives investigating the murder of a man stabbed to death at a shopping centre yards from the Olympic Park have charged nine people.Liam Woodards, 24, was knifed in a fight at Westfield in Stratford, east London, last Friday. Scotland Yard said three men who were originally arrested and six men who were detained over the weekend had been charged with violent disorder.Woodards, of Stratford, was pronounced dead at the scene of the attack, which happened in an area of the shopping centre called The Street. A postmortem revealed he died from a stab wound.
