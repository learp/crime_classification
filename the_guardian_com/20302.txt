A 28-year-old man has been charged by counter-terrorism officers over alleged fraud offences.Scotland Yard said Muhammed Saleem, from Abbey Wood, London – who was arrested on Saturday with two others – was accused of being in possession or controlling an article for use in fraud.He has been remanded in custody and is due to appear at Westminster magistrates court on Monday.The two other people arrested – a 33-year-old man and a 30-year-old woman – remain in custody at a central London police station, police said.A search in east London continues.
