Police have launched a murder investigation after a 26-year-old woman, believed to be a nurse, was stabbed to death outside a hospital in Blackpool.Officers received a call shortly before 8.30pm last night reporting that the woman had been attacked as she approached her car in the staff car park of Blackpool Victoria hospital.She was taken to the hospital's accident and emergency department but had died from her injuries by the time officers arrived.Lancashire police have appealed for witnesses to help them trace a white man, described as being in his 30s and stocky.Police believe the woman's attacker, who may have been on a bicycle, was known to her. Detective Superintendent Ian Critchley said: "We have launched a murder investigation following this incident and I would appeal for anyone with information to get in touch with us as a matter of urgency."A spokesman for the hospital said: "We can confirm that an incident occurred in the grounds of Blackpool Victoria hospital which resulted in a female being seriously injured. She was taken to A&E where she unfortunately died of her injuries."The police have been informed and are investigating."Anyone with information should call Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111 or Blackpool police on 01253 407 236.
