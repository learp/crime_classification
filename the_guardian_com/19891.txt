Dior jewellery worth £250,000 has gone missing from a London fashion shoot with Hollywood star Lindsay Lohan, police revealed today.The 22-year-old actor modelled the diamond necklace and earrings for Elle magazine at Big Sky studios in Holloway, north London, on 6 June.Staff from the magazine reported the jewellery missing two days later. It was loaned to Elle by the fashion house Dior. Lohan and 12 Elle employees who were also present at the shoot could face questioning by detectives.A Scotland Yard spokesman said: "We had an allegation of theft made to us on 8 June and that is being investigated."There have been no arrests yet. We want to speak to a number of people in connection with the inquiry."It is alleged it is a necklace and earrings with diamonds, with an estimated value of £250,000."
