Four youths, three of them 18 and one aged 17, were arrested yesterday as police launched a murder investigation following the death of a 17-year-old who was attacked as he walked home from a birthday party in Taunton, Somerset.Police said Lloyd Fouracre left the party at a social club with friends. They were approached by up to six people at about 12.30am and a fight broke out. Lloyd was injured and died in Musgrove Park hospital. Others were hurt, but not seriously. Detective Superintendent Arthur Lewis said: "This was a violent assault where a young man has lost his life."
