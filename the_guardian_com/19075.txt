A woman appeared in court today charged with murdering a man who fell under a tube train while wearing women's clothes.David Burgess, 63, from central London, was struck by a train at King's Cross station during the Monday evening rush hour.Nina Kanagasingham, 34, spoke only to confirm her name and date of birth when she appeared at a brief hearing at City of Westminster magistrates court.There was no application for bail and Kanagasingham, of Cricklewood, north-west London, was remanded in custody to appear at the Old Bailey on Monday.
