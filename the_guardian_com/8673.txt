A neighbour heard Toni-Ann Byfield's screams as the seven-year-old was shot alongside her father Bertram, the Old Bailey heard yesterday, on the second day of the trial of Joel Smith, 32, of no fixed abode, who pleads not guilty to their murders.Eumara David, who lived above the west London bedsit where the Byfields were murdered, said she had heard an argument begin in the flat below. "I heard four bangs. I have never heard a gun fire before, now I know it was four bullets. I heard the little girl scream. Then the front door hit the wall and it was quiet." The trial continues.
