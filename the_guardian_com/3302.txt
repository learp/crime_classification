A 24-year-old man has been shot dead in Birmingham, West Midlands police said today. The man, who has not been named, was shot last night in the Newtown area. He was taken to hospital where he was later pronounced dead.It is the third fatal shooting in the area in less than a year. On March 18 Nehemiah Bryce, 27, of Winson Green, was shot dead a few hundred metres from last night's attack. On December 23 last year, Assar Joseph Mohammed Tomlinson was killed during a row in Newtown.Last night's shooting took place in Hockley Close. A spokesman for West Midlands Ambulance Service said they had received a call at 10.13pm. "An ambulance was on scene within three minutes of the call and was backed up by a rapid response vehicle," he said. "A 24-year-old man was taken on blue lights to City hospital. The paramedic from the response vehicle travelled with the ambulance crew to provide additional assistance as the man was treated en route to hospital. "Sadly, despite the best efforts of ambulance and hospital staff it was not possible to save the man and he was confirmed dead at hospital."
