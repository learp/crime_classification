The husband of a newlywed woman found strangled at her home has been charged with murder. A police spokeswoman said: "The 26-year-old-man arrested in Wrexham on Sunday in connection with the police investigation into the death of Victoria Roberts at her home in Runcorn, Cheshire has this evening been charged with her murder." Michael Roberts was expected to appear in court this morning. The body of 25-year-old Vicky Roberts was discovered in a garage at her home in Runcorn, Cheshire, on Thursday night. The couple were married in June.
