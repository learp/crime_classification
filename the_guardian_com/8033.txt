Police tonight charged a man with burglary and robbery at the home of Steven Gerrard. The Liverpool and England midfielder's wife Alex Curran, 27, was confronted by four masked raiders at the couple's mansion in Formby, Merseyside, on the evening of 11 December 2007.She was left shaken but uninjured after the robbery that took place while Gerrard, 29, was playing in Liverpool's Champions League tie at Marseille.The offenders made off with items of jewellery.A Merseyside Police spokesman said: "Police have arrested and charged a 22-year-old man from Stockbridge Village, Liverpool, with burglary and robbery following an incident at a house in Formby on 11 December 2007. Martin Wilson will appear at North Sefton Magistrates' Court on 30 October."
