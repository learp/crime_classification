A 62-year-old woman was yesterday raped in her north Belfast home by an intruder who forced his way in after she answered a knock at the door.Officers say she was left severely traumatised after being subjected to a prolonged assault.Her attacker is believed to be in his late 20s or early 30s. Described as slim with short, dark hair, he was wearing blue jeans, a grey jacket and a white and pink T-shirt. Police have appealed to the public for any information.
