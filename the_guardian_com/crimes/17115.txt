A father-of-three was stabbed to death on his way home from a community fun day in Bristol yesterday. The 29-year-old appeared to have become involved in an argument after leaving a family event at a youth club.The attack comes amid mounting concern after a series of stabbings. Eyewitness Dominic Costello, 31, said: "When I got there I could see the man had managed to stumble inside his house. He was covered in blood. He had been stabbed in the neck and chest." A married father-of-one, Marlvin Jiro, 26, was stabbed to death in Birmingham on Monday.
