The death of a 19-year-old in a knife attack at a north London bedsit brings the number of teenagers killed in London this year to 20. They are: January 1 Henry Bolombi, 17, dies from a single stab wound after being attacked in Edmonton, north London. January 5 Faridon Alizada, 18, dies from chest wounds after being stabbed in a tower block in Erith, south-east London. January 21 Boduka Mudianga, 18, stabbed to death in a street brawl in Edmonton. January 26 Fuad Buraleh, 19, dies from a head injury after being beaten up in Ealing, west London. February 19 Sunday Essiet, 15, dies from a stab wound following a row between groups of youths in Woolwich, south-east London. February 23 Tung Le, 17, dies from a single stab wound during a row outside a West End nightclub. February 29 Ofiyke Nmezu, 16, dies two weeks after being hit on the head with a brick during an attack in Enfield, north-east London.March 13 Michael Jones, 18, dies from head and chest wounds after being attacked by an intruder at his home in Edmonton. March 14 Nicholas Clarke, 19, dies from a gunshot wound to the head in Brixton, south London. March 27 Devoe Roach, 17, dies after being stabbed in the chest in Stamford Hill, north London. March 27 Amro Elbadawi, 14, dies from a stab wound in Queen's Park, west London. May 3 Lyle Tulloch, 15, is fatally stabbed in a stairway of a block of flats in Borough, south-east London. May 10 Jimmy Mizen, 16, stabbed in the neck with a shard of glass in a bakery in Lee, south-east London. May 24 Rob Knox, 18, is stabbed to death in a fight outside a bar in Sidcup, south-east London. May 28 Sharmaarke Hassan, 17, dies four days after being shot in the head in Camden, north London. June 2 Arsema Dawit, 15, is stabbed to death in a lift in a block of flats near Waterloo station. June 29 Ben Kinsella, 16, dies after he is stabbed in Islington, north London. July 3 Shakilus Townsend, 16, dies after being stabbed and beaten with a baseball bat in Thorton Heath, south London. July 7 David Idowu, 14, dies three weeks after being stabbed outside a pub in Borough, south-east London. July 10 A 19-year-old is fatally stabbed in an attack at a bedsit in Edmonton.
