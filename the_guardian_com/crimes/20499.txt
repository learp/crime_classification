A man was arrested on suspicion of murder yesterday after a 59-year-old woman was stabbed to death in her employer's home in St Ives.The woman, a cleaner, was stabbed several times after surprising an intruder. Her body was found around three hours later by her elderly employer, who lives alone in the bungalow in Carbis Bay.Police believe she was the victim of a botched burglary. Detective Chief Inspector Mike Fowkes, who is leading the investigation, said it was likely the victim confronted or surprised an intruder, though police could find no signs of forced entry at the bungalow.
