Police in south London arrested two more teenagers this morning in connection with the murder of ten-year-old Damilola Taylor.The two black youths, aged 13 and 14, were arrested in Peckham, south-east London, and are being held at Southwark police station. Neither of the boys had previously been arrested, and the move brings the total number of arrests in the case to 15. In December, police arrested 11 youths in an early morning sweep of London, then later arrested another teenager and a 39-year-old woman. Two of those arrested were released unconditionally. The rest have been released on bail.Damilola, a Nigerian schoolboy, bled to death after being stabbed on his way home from a computer lesson in November.
