Two Thai fishermen yesterday pleaded guilty to raping and murdering the Welsh backpacker Katherine Horton on Lamai beach, Koh Samui, on January 1.Bualoi Posit, 23, and Wichai Somkhaoyai, 24, were whisked into a court in Surat Thani for a special hearing after the government ordered the case to be accelerated to reduce adverse publicity.A dozen witnesses are due to be heard today and the judges might also pass sentence, which could be death by lethal injection.A memorial service for Ms Horton, 21, was held yesterday on the beach where she was attacked.
