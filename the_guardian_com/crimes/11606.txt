A 30-year-old man today appeared in court charged with the murder of Barry Wilson, who was stabbed to death on the doorstep of his home.Keith Hunt is accused of murdering the father of three outside his house in the Knowle West area of the city on Tuesday afternoon. Mr Hunt, from Knowle, spoke only to confirm his name, address and date of birth during a 10-minute hearing at Bristol magistrates court.He was remanded in custody and will appear at Bristol crown court for a preliminary hearing on June 9. No plea was entered, and no application was made for bail.Mr Wilson, 29, was stabbed in the neck and through the heart as he made his way home from a police-sponsored family fun day. He was taken to Bristol Royal Infirmary, where he was later pronounced dead. His partner, Maxine Prescott, is pregnant with their fourth child.Mr Hunt is also charged with four other offences of driving a car without insurance, licence or test certificate between June and August last year.
