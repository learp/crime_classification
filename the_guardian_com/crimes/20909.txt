Two fishermen from the Isle of Lewis have been remanded in custody after appearing at Inverness sheriff court accused of murdering Liam Aitchison, a 16-year-old fisherman from South Uist.Johnathan Mackinnon, 21, from Plasterfield in Stornoway, and Stefan Miller, 20, from Gead Gorm, Carloway, were jointly charged over the weekend with murdering Aitchison in Stornoway towards the end of last month. His body was found on 29 November in a derelict government property close to Stornoway airport.The two made no plea or declaration.
