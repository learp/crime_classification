A 30-year-old man will appear in court later today charged with the murder of a baby in Northern Ireland.Millie Martin died in the Royal Victoria hospital in Belfast on Friday after being attacked and badly beaten at a house in County Fermanagh.The 15-month-old suffered severe injuries including a broken back.Detectives are investigating the possibility that the child was sexually assaulted.The man, believed to come from the Irvinestown area, is expected to appear at Omagh magistrates court.In a statement released on Saturday, Millie's family said they were suffering "unbearable grief" and were "heartbroken" and "numb" with pain.
