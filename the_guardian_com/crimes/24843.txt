Two schoolboys have been arrested by police investigating allegations that a 15-year-old girl was raped in a Birmingham secondary school.The boys, aged 14 and 15, have been released on bail pending further inquiries. They have been excluded from the school by the city's education authority.The attack is said to have taken place between January and April this year but the girl reported the incident to detectives only two weeks ago. Birmingham council confirmed that the pupils had been suspended. Neither the school nor the pupils have been named.A city council spokesman said: "This matter is subject to current police proceedings and the school is fully supporting the police investigation. The safety of all our young people is of paramount importance. Birmingham city council will provide support to staff and pupils at the school when the summer term commences."
