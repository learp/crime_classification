Bones discovered in a bag at the side of a motorway slip road are human remains, police said today.A workman was clearing vegetation on the slip road at junction 14 on the M5, north of Bristol, yesterday when he made the discovery.After examination by a pathologist today, Avon and Somerset police said the bones were human.A police spokesman said the death was being dealt with as "suspicious". Earlier, police had said there were a "significant" number of bones at the scene. The remains are understood to include arm, leg, pelvis and skull bones.But he said: "They cannot say what is the sex or age of the remains, or how long the bones had been there."An area stretching back into woodland at the side of the slip road remained cordoned off today.
