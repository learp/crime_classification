CCTV pictures captured the moment when shoppers were confronted by a man with an axe during an armed robbery.The masked man raises his axe to threaten two women as other shoppers scatter from a second robber with a sawn-off shotgun.The pictures, released yesterday, were taken at the B&Q store in Leigh, Greater Manchester, on August 27. Shoppers fled the store when the two men burst in demanding staff hand over money. The gunman pointed his weapon at staff and customers while the axeman emptied the till. The men escaped in an Alfa Romeo, Greater Manchester police said.
