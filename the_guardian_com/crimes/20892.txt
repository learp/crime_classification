A man appeared before Sevenoaks magistrates in Kent yesterday charged with the murder of Terry Edmonds, 17. Philip Bell, 21, of no fixed abode, is charged with murdering her between April 16 and 30.Bell was remanded in custody to appear before Maidstone crown court for a preliminary hearing on August 21. No pleas were entered and no bail application was made.Terry, from Hadlow, Kent, was last seen after she got off a train at Tunbridge Wells at 6.25pm on April 17. She lived in a nearby Christian homeless hostel. Her body was found in a green suitcase 12 days later.
