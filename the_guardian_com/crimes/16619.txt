Detectives investigating the murder of model Sally Anne Bowman, 18, in Croydon, south London, have written to 4,000 men in the area asking them for voluntary DNA samples.Ms Bowman was stabbed and sexually assaulted in the driveway of her home in the early hours of last September 25 by a suspected serial sex attacker who assaulted another woman in nearby Purley five years previously.Officers believe the assailant may have targeted other women. Police are urging all white or light-skinned men aged 20-40 in the area to give mouth swab and fingerprint samples.
