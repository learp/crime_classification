A woman has been arrested in connection with the death of a four-year-old girl whose body was found in the grounds of Raigmore hospital in Inverness.The discovery was made after a woman and a child were reported missing at around 10pm on Tuesday night in Inverness, prompting a major police search.The young girl was found dead shortly after 3am on Wednesday at the rear of the hospital. Northern Constabulary confirmed a 43-year-old woman had been arrested.Police had earlier appealed for anyone who saw a woman walking with a small girl near the hospital between 7pm on Tuesday and the early hours of Wednesday to get in touch.Around 10 forensics officers scoured the area on Wednesday afternoon, including nearby bins and bushes. The area was cordoned off with yellow police tape. Uniformed police officers were at the scene, which is away from the main hospital building.Raigmore hospital is an acute general hospital with 577 beds and it employs more than 3,000 staff. It is also a clinical teaching hospital for the University of Aberdeen.
