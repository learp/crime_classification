Police have launched a murder investigation after a 30-year-old man was stabbed to death.Emergency services were called to a flat in Clapton, east London, on Saturday night where they found a man with stab wounds. He was pronounced dead at the scene.Scotland Yard said three men had been involved in an argument outside the flat at Lea View House on Jessam Avenue hours earlier.Two men – a 45-year-old who is in police custody and a 29-year-old man who is in hospital with non-life threatening stab wounds – have been arrested on suspicion of murder.The dead man's next of kin have been informed but he is yet to be formally identified. A postmortem will take place at a later date.Officers want to speak to anyone who was in the areas of Jessam Avenue between 3pm and 9pm yesterday.
