A 53-year-old man is recovering in hospital after he was stabbed in the throat on his doorstep.The victim was attacked on Saturday night after answering the door to a man who claimed to be looking for something in the garden of his home in Bingley, West Yorkshire.He was taken to Bradford Royal Infirmary, where he remained in a stable condition.West Yorkshire police are appealing for anyone with information to contact them.The attack happened at about 10.15pm after the victim noticed someone in his garden. He spoke to the man, who apologised for disturbing the householder and his wife and said he was looking for an item lost by his brother.The victim lent the man a torch and asked him to leave it on the doorstep when he had finished searching. About 10 minutes later, the victim answered a knock at the door and was stabbed in the throat.The attacker was described as white, 6ft tall and aged about 20.
