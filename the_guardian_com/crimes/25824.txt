A 14-year-old girl has appeared in court charged with the murder of a 15-year-old boy. A court official said another male, aged 16, was also at Camberwell Green youth court on Monday accused of killing the promising young footballer Junior Nkwelle in south London last week.The pair, who cannot be named for legal reasons, will next appear at the Old Bailey on Wednesday.Junior was stabbed to death outside a block of flats on the Loughborough Estate in Brixton last Thursday evening. He was pronounced dead at the scene, with police saying the killing was not believed to be gang-related. Junior has been described as a "role model" for his community. A vigil was held on Sunday night for the victim.
