Two men have been charged with the murder of a teenager who was shot in London last month.Kyle McDonald, 19, from Fulham, was killed on 18 September in Battersea, south-west London.Noor Omar, 23, of St John's Hill, south-west London, and Darrell Michael Giscombe, 23, of Weekley Square, also south-west London, will appear in custody at Hendon magistrates court on Monday, the Metropolitan police said.Police believe McDonald may have sustained his injuries in Musjid Road before collapsing in Coppock Close, where he was found by officers. A post-mortem examination confirmed he died from a single gunshot wound.
