A man who drove his motorcycle over a police officer as she broke up a fight was jailed for four years yesterday.Wayne Hewitt of Pennington, Hampshire, was sentenced at Southampton crown court for dangerous driving and wounding with intent to cause grievous bodily harm. The 36-year-old was also disqualified from driving for six years.A trial last month heard that Hewitt rode his motorbike over PC Liz Parsonage outside the Thomas Tripp pub in Lymington, Hampshire, in January. The attack took place as the officer attempted to break up a fight outside the premises.
