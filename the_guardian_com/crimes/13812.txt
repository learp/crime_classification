A young man was critically ill in hospital last night after he was stabbed yesterday by two men on a London bus.The victim, who is in his twenties, was travelling on the number 29 bus in Mornington Crescent, near Regent's Park, when the attack took place after a confrontation with the men. He was taken to hospital where he was in a critical but stable condition.A police spokesman said one man was arrested at the scene, but the second escaped. He is described as aged between 20 and 25, black and 6ft tall. He was wearing dark clothing and made off towards Eversholt Street.Anyone who witnessed the attack, which took place at about 5.30am, is asked to telephone the police on 020 8733 6573.
