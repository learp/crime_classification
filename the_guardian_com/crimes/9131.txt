Two men are in hospital after being stabbed at London's Wireless music festival in east London on Sunday, police said.The attack happened at about 9pm while headline acts Justin Timberlake and Jay-Z were performing in front of tens of thousands of people.One man in his 20s was reported to be in a critical condition with the other man, also believed to be in his 20s, said to be in a stable condition. No arrests have been made.The three-day festival at the Queen Elizabeth park on the Olympic site came to a close shortly before midnight.
