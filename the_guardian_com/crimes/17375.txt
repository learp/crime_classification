A man arrested yesterday morning by police investigating the abduction and murder of Milly Dowler was last night released without charge.The 57-year-old from London, a registered sex offender, was arrested by officers from Surrey police at 8am and questioned over the 13-year-old's killing in 2002. He is the fifth person to have been detained in connection with the murder. Milly went missing near Walton-on-Thames station on March 21 2002 as she was returning home from school. Her body was eventually found in woods at Yately Heath, Hampshire, six months later.
