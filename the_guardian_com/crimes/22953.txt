Gangs of youths who go "trick or treating" in ski or Scream-style masks and terrorise their neighbourhoods are facing a police crackdown.Figures show a rise in robbery around the time of Halloween in London, with trick or treating often used as a cover by criminals. Scotland Yard is to step up patrols over Halloween this year.Assistant Commissioner Tim Godwin, in charge of London's Safer Neighbourhoods teams, said there was a big difference between children trick or treating under adult supervision, and gangs in ski masks knocking on their neighbours' doors and demanding treats.
