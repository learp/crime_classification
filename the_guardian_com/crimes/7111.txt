Four teenagers were remanded in custody by Huddersfield magistrates yesterday accused of the murder of taxi driver Mohammed Pervaiz.Michael David Hand, 19, and three others who cannot be named for legal reasons are charged with killing the 41-year-old in what police described as a racially aggravated incident. Hand and two 17-year-olds were remanded to Leeds prison and a 16-year-old to secure accommodation.West Yorkshire police yesterday arrested another 16-year-old on suspicion of the murder and are still questioning an 18-year-old who was held on Tuesday night.
