The detective who led the inquiry into the crimes of Fred and Rose West is to reveal the inside story of the police investigation for the first time in a book to be published next month to coincide with the 10th anniversary of Rose West's conviction.John Bennett, who is now retired from the Gloucestershire force, will describe in The Cromwell Street Murders how the serial killers were caught and the huge impact the horrific story had on all involved.Mr Bennett was discreet about the inquiry during the trial of Rose West, which followed the suicide in prison of Fred West.
