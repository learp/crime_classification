A woman was tonight charged after police found the remains of four babies at her home.The decomposed remains were discovered by officers from Merseyside police during an investigation last September.Grandmother Bernadette Quirk, 55, formerly of Harlow Close, St Helens, Merseyside, has been charged with "concealment of birth", police said.A spokesman said: "Bernadette Quirk, formerly of Harlow Close, has been bailed to appear at St Helens magistrates court on Thursday 17 June."
