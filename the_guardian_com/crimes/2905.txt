A 30-year old woman has been arrested in Hackney, east London, on suspicion of murdering two children, a three-year-old girl and a 10-year-old boy.Police were called to a house in Pembury Close yesterday after they reports of the deaths of two children. An ambulance went to the address, but the children were pronounced dead at the scene.Police believe they know the identity of the children, but are waiting for formal identification and to inform next of kin before releasing their names. A spokesman said police were not looking for anyone else in connection with the murders.Detectives from the Child Abuse Investigation Team were leading the investigation, the spokesman said. The woman remained in custody last night.
