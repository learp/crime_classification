Police have launched a murder investigation after a man was stabbed to death during a disturbance in a residential street in Bristol.The man, believed to be in his 40s, suffered knife wounds in the head and chest during the altercation at about 9pm last night in the Withywood area of the city. He was taken to hospital in a critical condition and died this morning.Police have arrested two men in connection with the incident. A forensic examination was being carried out on the street where the stabbing took place."We believe there were a number of people in the Gatehouse Avenue area at the time of the incident who may have useful information to help our enquiries," a spokesman for Avon and Somerset police said.
