Two men appeared in court yesterday accused of murdering gay barman Jody Dobrowski.Unemployed Thomas Pickford, 25, and decorator Scott Walker, 33, were arrested on Friday night. They appeared at South Western magistrates' court in Clapham, east London, and spoke only to confirm their names, date of birth and say they were of no fixed abode. There was no application for bail and the two were remanded in custody to appear at the Old Bailey on January 31.Mr Dobrowski, 24, was killed on Clapham Common on October 14 as he walked home.
