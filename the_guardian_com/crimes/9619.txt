The former broadcaster Stuart Hall, who was jailed in July for indecent assault, is to face questioning over fresh allegations of child sex abuse.Lancashire police have confirmed they are working with the Crown Prosecution Service after the claims were made this summer."We have had further allegations put to us and we are working with the CPS on how to go forward with the investigation," said a spokeswoman.The 83-year-old, from Wilmslow, Cheshire, was jailed in July for 15 months after admitting 14 counts of indecent assault against girls as young as nine between 1967 and 1987.His sentence was doubled by the court of appeal, with the lord chief justice, Lord Judge, describing the original sentence as too lenient.Hall is reported to be facing five further allegations.
