Two teenagers are in a serious condition in hospital today after a double shooting in London.Police said the youths, aged 18 and 19, were taken to separate hospitals after being shot last night in Neasden, north-west London.Police said the shootings were linked but the two youths were not believed to have shot at each other.Officers and the London Ambulance Service were called to the scene at Blackbird Hill, Neasden, where they found the two men with gunshot wounds.A Metropolitan police spokeswoman said: "They are in a serious but stable condition. Officers from the Metropolitan police service shootings team are investigating."The spokeswoman said no arrests had yet been made in connection with the shooting.Police are advising motorists to avoid the area today due to road closures as officers search the scene.
