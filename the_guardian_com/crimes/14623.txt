Comedy actor Chris Langham today pleaded not guilty to 24 charges of sexual offences.The comedian appeared in court today accused of assaulting a girl and having her perform oral sex on him at a top London theatre. The star of BBC show The Thick of It pleaded not guilty to eight counts of indecent assault and one of buggery against a girl under 16 between April 1994 and April 1998. He also denied 15 counts of making indecent photos of children between September and November 2005.Langham, of Cranbrook in Kent, stood at Maidstone Crown Court for a preparatory hearing into the sexual assault charges. The award-winning comedian, who has five children, was charged with the child porn offences last May as part of police child porn investigation, Operation Ore.
