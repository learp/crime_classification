A man was tonight charged with the brutal murder of an elderly widow.Joan Charlton, 85, was found dead at her home in West Parade, Hull, on Thursday.Detectives said she had suffered a "sustained and brutal attack" in which every bone in her face was broken.A member of the public raised the alarm on Thursday afternoon and Mrs Charlton's body was found in the terraced house where she lived alone.Tonight, Humberside police said Robert Michael Sanders, 20, from Hull, had been charged with Mrs Charlton's murder.Sanders remains in custody and will appear before Hull magistrates court tomorrow.
