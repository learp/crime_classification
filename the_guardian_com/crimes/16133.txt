A 53-year-old man was remanded in custody today when he appeared in court accused of murdering a mother-to-be.Alan McMullan appeared at Grimsby magistrates court for a hearing which lasted just two minutes.Claire Wilson, 21, from Grimsby, north-east Lincolnshire, died along with her unborn child after she was stabbed in the back, in the town on Sunday afternoon.McMullan, of Sherwood Road, Grimsby, was remanded in custody to appear at Hull crown court on 16 June .There was no application for bail.
