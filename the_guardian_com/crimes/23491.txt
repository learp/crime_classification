A 39-year-old man appeared in court today charged with the kidnapping and false imprisonment of Shannon Matthews.Michael Donovan, who allegedly snatched the nine-year-old schoolgirl on February 19, was remanded in custody from Dewsbury magistrates court.The Dewsbury man has been ordered to appear at Leeds crown court on March 25.Donovan was arrested on Friday when police found Shannon 24 days after she disappeared less than one mile from her home in Dewsbury, West Yorkshire.Shannon remains in the care of the police, who have said she is being questioned in sessions of up to 15 minutes.Shannon's mother, Karen Matthews, has seen her daughter briefly but the girl is not expected to return home for some time.
