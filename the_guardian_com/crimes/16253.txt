A 23-year-old man appeared in court today accused of the murder of a woman who was mown down with her own car.Lynda Hankey, 42, suffered fatal head injuries after she was thrown into the air off the bonnet of her yellow MG sports car.Hankey was allegedly trying to stop the car being driven off from outside her home in Worsley Mesnes, Wigan, on Thursday.Shaun Higgins, of Leigh, near Wigan, appeared before magistrates accused of murder and aggravated vehicle taking.He spoke only to confirm his name, address and date of birth.His solicitor, Peter Antrobus, made no application for bail and Higgins was remanded in custody.Chairman of the bench, Patrick Else, adjourned the case to 9 February at Liverpool crown court.
