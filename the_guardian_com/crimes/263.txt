Five people appeared at Horseferry Road magistrates' court, London, yesterday charged with kidnapping a boy, 10, last Thursday from his home in Walthamstow, east London.Dawn Fields, 25, and Jeriah Simpson, 20, of Clapham, south London, were charged with kidnapping, false imprisonment and blackmail. Damien Francis, 24, Gadwell Gibbs, 23, and Edwin Phillip, 21, all of New York, were charged with kidnapping, false imprisonment, blackmail, possession of a firearm and aggravated burglary.They were remanded in custody to appear at Middlesex Guildhall crown court on March 6.
