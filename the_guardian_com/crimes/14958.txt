A teenager has been arrested after eight people were stabbed in a town centre overnight.Police were called to Keighley, in West Yorkshire, after reports of a number of stabbings in two separate incidents.A 24-year-old man was stabbed at 10.15pm and is in a stable condition in hospital.Another seven people were injured near a Wetherspoons pub at 12.30am.Four of those – three women aged 26, 44 and 51, and a man aged 30 – have been admitted to hospital, where they were also said to be in a stable condition.The man arrested in connection with both incidents was detained by members of the public near the Wetherspoons bar.The mental health of the detained man is being assessed, police said.
