Almost a third of black and Asian people fear being subjected to police harassment if they make a complaint against their force, an Independent Police Complaints Commission survey says today.It found 30% of Asians feared police harassment if they complained, compared with 28% of black people and 18% of white people. The IPCC has looked at more than 350 serious cases since replacing the Police Complaints Authority two years ago. It found 90% of Asians would complain if an officer assaulted them during a demonstration, as opposed to 87% of white people and 82% of black people.
