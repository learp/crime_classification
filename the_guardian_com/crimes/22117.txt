Two men have been injured in a shooting at a farm and equestrian centre in central Scotland.Police were called to a property in Auldhouse, near East Kilbride in South Lanarkshire, at 8.10am.A force spokesman said: "Police are in attendance at a farm in East Kilbride following reports of a shooting incident. Extensive inquiries are ongoing."It is understood the two men were targeted and police say they are following a positive line of inquiry.The incident happened at Meadowhead Farm, a licensed equestrian centre that offers lessons and treks.A statement posted on the farm's Facebook page said all lessons and treks had been cancelled on Tuesday, and apologised for the inconvenience."
