A BMW car belonging to the Rangers midfielder DaMarcus Beasley has been set on fire in Glasgow. The incident happened on Monday, outside the American's home.Writing on Twitter today, Beasley said "someone blew up my car". He added that he was "glad no one got hurt".Strathclyde police is treating the incident as "wilful fire raising" and stated that inquiries are ongoing.Rangers have described it as a "disturbing and deplorable incident".
