A 16-year-old girl has been sexually assaulted while waiting at a bus stop. The assault happened in broad daylight at 3.30pm on Tuesday while several others were also waiting for the bus in Leamington Spa, Warwickshire, said police.A man sat next to the girl at a stop outside the Savers store, in Leamington Parade, and began whispering in her ear before inappropriately touching the teenager, according to Warwickshire police.Both victim and attacker then separately got on the same bus - the number 67 - with the man later getting off.The man is described as aged between 60 and 70, 5ft 9in tall, with short brown hair and a bald spot on top of his head. He was wearing glasses and a cream or beige-coloured suit.A spokesman for the force said "multiple people were waiting for the bus at the time and are believed to have witnessed the assault".Anyone with information is urged to call police on 101, or contact Crimestoppers on 0800 555111.
