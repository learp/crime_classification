A 78-year-old man has died in hospital after suffering head injuries during an attack at a Sikh temple on Christmas Eve, police said today.West Midlands police have launched a murder inquiry after the death of the pensioner, who has not been named, in the early hours of this morning."It's believed the offenders got into the temple and threatened and attacked the victim before stealing property," a police spokesman said. A post-mortem examination will take place later today. The victim was injured between 3am and 4am on December 24 at the Guru Nanak temple in Cannock Road, Wolverhampton. Police urge anyone with any information to contact Wednesfield CID on 0845 113 5000.
