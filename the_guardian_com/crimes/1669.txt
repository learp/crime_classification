A murder inquiry has been launched in Manchester after a 16-year-old boy was shot in the head outside a pub late last night.Police went to the Robin Hood pub in Barton Road, Stretford, after shots were reported just before midnight.The teenager, who has not yet been formally identified, was taken to Trafford general hospital but died shortly afterwards.Three men aged 18, 20 and 22 have been arrested on suspicion of murder and remain in police custody.Detective Chief Inspector Howard Millington, of Greater Manchester police, said inquiries were at a very early stage."We are urging anyone with any information to come forward," he said.
