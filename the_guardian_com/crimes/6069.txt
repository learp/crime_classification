The British woman found buried in a bath of sand in Tokyo died from asphyxiation caused by strangulation, Japanese police said today.The body of 22-year-old Lindsay Ann Hawker, from Brandon, near Coventry, was discovered on the balcony of a flat in the east of the capital on Monday night. Police have issued an arrest warrant for 28-year-old Tatsuya Ichihashi, who was seen fleeing the scene barefoot. Miss Hawker's father, Bill, and her boyfriend, Ryan Garside, flew to Japan to formally identify the body. Mr Hawker said he "would not rest" until his daughter's killer was found.
