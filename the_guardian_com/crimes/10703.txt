A teenage girl will appear before magistrates today, charged with murdering her 16-year-old sister.The 14-year-old - accused of stabbing her sister to death at their home in Halifax, West Yorkshire - will appear at Bradford magistrates court.A West Yorkshire police spokesman said the girl was charged with murder yesterday and had been kept in custody. He added that she would appear before Calderdale magistrates, who are temporarily sitting at the Bradford court, this morning.Police and paramedics were called to an address in the Ovenden area of the town on Monday night, where Yorkshire Ambulance Service said two teenage girls were found with stab wounds.Both were taken to Calderdale Royal Hospital, where the 16-year-old died. Her sister was later released from hospital and taken to a police station.West Yorkshire police have not yet made the results of a post mortem examination public.
