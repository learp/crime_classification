A nurse was remanded in custody yesterday after being charged with four murders and an attempted murder of patients at two hospitals in Leeds in 2002.Colin Norris, 29, spoke only to confirm his name and address in Glasgow before he was accused at Leeds magistrates court of killing Bridget Bourke, 88, Ethel Hall, 86, Doris Ludlam, 80, and Irene Crookes, 79.He was also charged with the attempted murder of Vera Wilby, 90. All the alleged victims were from the Leeds area. No plea was entered by Mr Norris, who will appear at Leeds crown court next Wednesday.
