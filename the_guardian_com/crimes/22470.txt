Four detectives and three crime scene investigators from Hampshire are aboard the luxury liner Queen Mary 2 investigating the death of a 40-year-old Filipino crew member on Friday.Hampshire police, who have jurisdiction because the ship is registered in Southampton, said the man became involved in a fight below decks and suffered head injuries.A 49-year-old Filipino has been detained on the ship by the captain under section 105 of the Merchant Shipping Act in connection with the death. The Hampshire police team flew to Norway and boarded the ship at Bergen on Saturday.
