A teenager is expected to appear in court today in connection with the death of Farah Noor Adams.The 18-year-old man was arrested yesterday by police who have been working on a murder inquiry since Ms Adams's body was discovered by workmen by the river Kelvin in Glasgow last Friday.Earlier that day, Ms Adams, 34, had dropped her eight-year-old daughter off at school and then gone for a power walk on the Kelvin walkway, a popular route along the banks of the river.Detectives have interviewed about 100 joggers, walkers and cyclists and have studied CCTV footage of the area.
