The family of a baby girl from Northern Ireland who died from horrific injuries released a statement tonight saying they are heartbroken over the death. Police are treating Millie Martin's death as murder with the killing raising questions about child protection in the western part of the Province. A spokesman for the Martin family said: "We simply can't believe that our loving, darling little angel is gone." Detectives are questioning a 30-year-old man about the death. The child died at the Royal Victoria Belfast on Friday. She had suffered a number of injuries at a house in Enniskillen.
