A woman has been charged with the murder of a 63-year-old man who fell under a tube train while wearing women's clothes.Nina Kanagasingham, 34, of Cricklewood in north London, will appear before City of Westminster magistrates tomorrow.The victim, from central London, was struck by a train at King's Cross underground station at rush hour on Monday evening.The station had to be evacuated and the Piccadilly line temporarily closed as police recovered the man's body.Officers viewed CCTV footage and interviewed witnesses at the station following the man's death.British Transport police stressed such incidents are "extremely rare". The man's name is yet to be released.
