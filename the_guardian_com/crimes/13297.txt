Five men have been arrested on suspicion of murder after a man was stabbed to death in West Yorkshire in the early hours of this morning.The 31-year-old man victim was killed in a disturbance in Bradford shortly after midnight. A 27-year-old man was seriously injured.Police were carrying out searches and house-to-house enquiries this morning and a postmortem examination is expected to take place later today.Five men aged between 19 and 26 were arrested on suspicion of murder and a 33-year-old man was arrested for violent disorder.The incident came at the end of after a bank holiday weekend of violence including at least two other killings.
