Police have launched an investigation after the bodies of a man and woman were discovered in a house in Staffordshire.The deaths are being treated as suspicious, Staffordshire police said. A police spokesman said the bodies were found at about 12.50pm on Monday at an address in Ness Grove, Cheadle.The spokesman said forensic examinations were being conducted at the scene, which had been sealed off.Anyone with relevant information is asked to contact Staffordshire police on the non-emergency 101 number.Alternatively, information can be passed on anonymously via Crimestoppers on 0800-555 111.
