A man has died and a woman has suffered life-threatening injuries at a house in Nottinghamshire, police have said.Two uninjured children were also found by police at the house in Galway Road, Bircotes, shortly before 1am on Friday.Officers were investigating the circumstances around the death of the man.A spokesman for Nottinghamshire police said: "The force was called to Galway Road at about 12.50am."A man was found dead at the property. A woman and two children were also at the property."The children have been safely removed and are uninjured."The woman has been taken to hospital with potentially life-threatening injuries."A cordon remained in place at the scene while the investigation was under way.
