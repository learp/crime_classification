A teenage mother of two and her boyfriend set fire to her devout Catholic father's house because he refused to let them sleep together, Northampton crown court heard yesterday.Bernadette McDermott, 18, and Dean Stone, 20, pleaded guilty to arson with intent to endanger life. They were remanded in custody for psychiatric reports.The couple locked the father, Noel, in his home in Wellingborough on January 7 last year, disabling fire alarms, and set fire to it. Mr McDermott escaped by jumping from his daughter's window before trying to force his way back in to rescue her.
