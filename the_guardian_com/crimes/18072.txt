A Metropolitan police officer has denied assaulting a black teenager who was in custody.PC Joseph Harrington, 28, pleaded not guilty to the charge of assault occasioning actual bodily harm as he stood in the dock at Westminster magistrates court in London.A spokesman for the Met confirmed that Harrington had been suspended from duty pending the outcome of the case.Harrington is alleged to have assaulted a 16-year-old boy at Forest Gate police station in east London on 11 August last year, days after the summer riots.Harrington will appear at Southwark crown court on 13 July. He was given unconditional bail.
