A 13-year-old boy appeared in court today, charged with possessing a firearm.Scotland Yard says the teenager may be the youngest ever to appear in court in London for firearms offences.In addition to charges of possession of a firearm with intent to endanger life, the boy is charged with possession of a prohibited weapon, namely an automatic handgun, and possession of ammunition. The boy was arrested after police investigating gang crime raided a house in New Cross Gate in south-east London.Officers found a converted Russian handgun, two silencers and bag of ammunition, Balham youth court was told.The boy was remanded in custody and will appear in court on August 27.
