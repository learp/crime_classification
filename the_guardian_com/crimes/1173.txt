Pete Doherty has been arrested on suspicion of stealing a car and possessing Class A drugs after police chased him allegedly driving the wrong way up a one-way street in Birmingham.The Babyshambles singer, 26, who is understood to own five secondhand Jaguars, was at the wheel of a blue three-litre Jaguar when he was stopped on Monday evening. Doherty was bailed after spending the night in custody.He is undertaking drug rehabilitation as part of a 12-month community service order and must report on his progress in court next week. He could face prison if he breaches the order.
