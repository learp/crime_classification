Four people were arrested today in connection with the murder of black teenager Anthony Walker, police said.Three men, aged 31, 25 and 18, and a 34-year-old woman, all from Liverpool, were arrested on suspicion of perverting the course of justice.Anthony, an 18-year-old A-Level student, was bludgeoned to death with an axe in a Merseyside park on July 29 this year.Paul Taylor, 20, and Michael Barton, 17, both from Huyton, have been charged with murder and are awaiting trial.A spokeswoman for Merseyside police said the three people arrested this morning were being questioned at a local police station.The 31-year-old man and the woman are both from the Kirkdale area and the 18-year-old-man is from Huyton.
