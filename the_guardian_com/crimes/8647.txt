A teenager appeared before Enfield magistrates in north London yesterday charged with the murder of nurse Cheryl Moss, who was stabbed to death outside the hospital where she worked.Trainee accountant Stuart Harling, 18, of Rainham, Essex, was remanded in custody. He spoke only to give his name, date of birth and address. There was no application for bail and Harling is to appear at the Old Bailey for a plea and directions hearing on July 17.Mrs Moss, 33, was killed on Thursday as she took a cigarette break outside St George's hospital in Hornchurch, Essex.
