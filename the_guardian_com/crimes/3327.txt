A Royal Navy submariner has appeared before a court accused of passing secrets to a foreign enemy.Edward Devenney, 29, from Strabane, County Tyrone, is charged with two offences under the Official Secrets Act. He is accused of collating information about programmes for secret codes that might be useful to an enemy, and communicating this material to another person.The petty officer appeared via videolink at the Old Bailey, where no plea was lodged, and he will face trial on 13 November.
