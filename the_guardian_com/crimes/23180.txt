A 49-year-old Sunderland man was charged yesterday with carrying out the notorious "I'm Jack" tape and letter hoax which disastrously diverted the hunt for the Yorkshire Ripper 27 years ago.A district judge in Leeds remanded John Humble in custody charged with perverting the course of justice during the hunt for the serial killer of 13 women, three of them killed after the hoax diverted police resources from Leeds and Bradford to the north-east of England. Peter Sutcliffe, from Bradford, was charged with the murders in 1981 and is now in Broadmoor.
