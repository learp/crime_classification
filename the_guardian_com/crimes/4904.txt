The jury in the trial of actor Chris Langham was expected to retire today to consider its verdicts.The 58-year-old Bafta-winning star is charged with having sex with an under-age girl and downloading child pornography. The jury of four women and seven men at Maidstone crown court spent yesterday listening to closing speeches by the judge, Philip Statman. A male juror was discharged on Tuesday on "administrative grounds".Mr Langham, a father of five from Golford, near Cranbrook, Kent, denies six charges of indecent assault and two charges of buggery between January 1996 and April 2000.He also denies 15 charges of making an indecent image of a child between September and November 2005.
