A teenager was arrested today over the double murder of Joan and John Stirland, who were shot dead in their Lincolnshire seaside bungalow earlier this month, police said.The 19-year-old, from Nottingham, will be interviewed by detectives in Lincolnshire later today.Mrs Stirland, 53, and her 55-year-old husband were in hiding in the resort of Trusthorpe after their Nottingham home was shot at following the conviction of their son, Michael O'Brien, for murder.O'Brien had shot dead an innocent man outside a pub in Nottingham. Police believe it is possible the couple's murder was a revenge attack.The pair fled their home in Carlton, Nottingham, on September 14 last year.
