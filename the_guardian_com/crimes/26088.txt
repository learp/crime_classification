The mother of four-year-old Chloe Fletcher, found dead at her Merseyside home yesterday, has been charged with her murder.Laura Fletcher, 22, was arrested after police were called to the house in Wallasey early yesterday morning. A postmortem has revealed the cause of death as drowning.In a photograph released today by police, Chloe is seen outdoors wearing a white sundress and clutching a biscuit. Residents of the street where she lived with her mother have spoken of their shock after police cordoned off the family's semi-detached house and began door-to-door murder inquiries.Meanwhile, dozens of people have posted tributes and messages mourning Chloe's death on the website Gone Too Soon.Fletcher was due to appear at Wirral magistrates court this afternoon.
