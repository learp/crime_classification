So Solid Crew's Megaman is facing a third murder trial after an Old Bailey jury was discharged yesterday when "fresh information" came to light.The rapper, real name Dwayne Vincent, is alleged to have urged his friend, Carl Morgan, to shoot Colin Scarlett in a revenge attack in November 2004.Morgan - another member of the rap collective - was jailed for life last year after he was convicted of shooting Mr Scarlett for showing him "disrespect."
