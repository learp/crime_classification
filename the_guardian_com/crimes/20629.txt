Police have made three arrests following a murder outside a school in north-west London. Shawn Callum, 26, from Maida Vale, was gunned down outside Stonbridge school in the early hours of February 8; it is believed that he had been attending a party near the school, and was shot as he left the event. Three men aged 18, 21 and 23 were last night in custody at north London police stations, having earlier been arrested on suspicion of murder. Callum's death is being investigated by Operation Trident, the Metropolitan police unit set up to end shootings and killings in London's black community.
