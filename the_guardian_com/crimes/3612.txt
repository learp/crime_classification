A gardener was charged yesterday with the murder of a woman whose body was discovered in woods days after she went missing. Fingerprint matches were used to identify Linda Casey, 54, who was found in Banstead Woods, Surrey, last Thursday. Peter Ling, 54, from Wallington, south London, was arrested in Somerset. He was remanded in custody to appear at the Old Bailey on 1 September.A postmortem at Royal Surrey county hospital in Guildford found serious head injuries, but could not establish the cause of death. Surrey police were today continuing their inquiries around Banstead Woods and the car park at a nearby pub where Ms Casey was believed to have been between 10am and 6pm last Saturday.
