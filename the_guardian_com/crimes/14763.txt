A special Waking The Dead-style police unit in Northern Ireland – specialising in unsolved or "cold" cases – has charged a 37-year-old man with a murder committed 12 years ago.The man is appear before Belfast magistrates court later today accused of the murder of John Harbinson in May 1997.He was arrested by officers from the historical enquiries team on Tuesday. The unit is tasked with reopening cases connected to the Troubles.Harbinson died after being handcuffed and beaten by a loyalist gang on the Mount Vernon estate in north Belfast.In July, a loyalist, Mark Haddock, appeared in court also accused of Harbinson's murder. He denied the charge.
