Three men were stabbed and 19 others arrested after a fight broke out in Trafalgar Square yesterday.Police are linking the stabbings and said those involved in the brawl, which broke out in the London tourist spot at around 4pm, were believed to be from eastern Europe.The three injured men were being treated in hospital, the Metropolitan Police said. Two victims, aged 18 and 19, suffered multiple stab wounds. One is in a stable condition and the other is serious but stable.A third man, aged 19, admitted himself to hospital with stab wounds and was in a serious but stable condition.
