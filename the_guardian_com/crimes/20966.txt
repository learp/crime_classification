A 19-year-old man was in a critical condition in hospital today after becoming the latest teenager to be stabbed in a London street.The man was found at East Ham underground station in east London at about 9.30pm yesterday evening, police said.A second man, also aged 19, was arrested in connection with the incident after he was found with head injuries in nearby Burgess Road. He is in hospital where his condition is described as not life threatening.Detectives believe there was a dispute in the street near the station which resulted in the two men, who are both believed to be Sri Lankan or Tamil, being injured.The incident came only hours after the parents of teenage actor Robert Knox – who was stabbed to death on Friday in Sidcup, south-east London – appealed to people not to carry knives. Knox was the 14th teenager to be murdered in London this year.
