The bodies of two men have been found in the back garden of a property in Hull.Humberside police were called at 6.04pm on Friday, where the two men were discovered.The deaths are being treated as suspicious but unexplained, police said.A police statement added: “A cordon is in place at the scene and inquiries are under way to establish the identity of the men and the circumstance of their deaths.“Detectives are appealing for the local community’s help and would like to speak to anyone with information about the incident.Anyone who can assist police are urged to call 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
