Brian Regan, a former soap star, has been charged with the murder of a nightclub doorman who was gunned down in the street, police said today.Regan, 53, who played Terry Sullivan in the Liverpool-based Channel 4 drama Brookside, is accused of the murder of Bahman Faraji.Faraji was shot dead outside a pub in Aigburth, Liverpool, on 24 February.Detectives have also charged Lee George Dodson, 42, of Logfield Drive, Garston, with the murder of Faraji, a father-of-one who lived in Toxteth.Regan, of Garston, Liverpool, also faces an allegation of perverting the course of justice.Both men will appear before Liverpool magistrates later today.
