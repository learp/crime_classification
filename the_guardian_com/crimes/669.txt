Two brothers who saw themselves as 21st century Kray twins were each jailed yesterday for a minimum of 26 years for the murder of a millionaire garage owner and his wife.Lee and Robert Firkins, 31 and 33, shot Graham and Carol Fisher three times each with sawn-off shotguns before bludgeoning the couple with sledgehammers in a raid on a garage near Wadebridge, Cornwall. The brothers, originally from Essex, committed a string of other offences, including robbery and assaults.The judge at Exeter crown court, Mr Justice Owen, said: "They are extremely violent and dangerous men."
