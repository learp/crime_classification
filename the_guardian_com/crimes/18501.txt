Police have charged a 20-year-old man with the murder of a rush-hour commuter who was pushed in front of a tube train. The victim, a man in his 50s, was pushed from the Victoria line platform as a train pulled into Highbury and Islington station in north London on Friday evening.Officers arrested a suspect early on Saturday, questioned him overnight and charged him yesterday. He is due before Horseferry Road magistrates in central London today.British Transport police have appealed for information from witnesses, particularly those who chased a man from the station after the incident.
