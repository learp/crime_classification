A 15-year-old boy has been arrested on suspicion of robbing a bank at gunpoint.A branch of Barclays bank in Breck Road, Liverpool, was held up just after 11.50am on Friday.A Merseyside police spokesman said: "The 15-year-old male, from the Anfield area, has been taken into custody where he will be questioned."Police said the thief entered the bank and threatened staff with a gun before fleeing with cash.Staff inside the bank were unhurt but have been left distressed by their ordeal, police added.
