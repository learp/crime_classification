Three men were charged with conspiracy to commit robbery yesterday in connection with a raid in which PC Sharon Beshenivsky was shot dead.The men, all 24, have been charged in connection with the incident at Universal Express travel agents on November 18 last year. PC Beshenivsky, 38, was shot dead after responding to an alarm call at the premises. Her colleague PC Teresa Milburn was also shot and injured.The men were arrested last week in London and will appear at Leeds magistrates court today. Detectives want to question Mustaf Jama, 25, in connection with the shooting.
