A man appeared in court today charged with the brutal murder of an elderly widow.Joan Charlton, who was 85, was found dead last Thursday at her terrace house in West Parade, close to the centre of Hull, where she lived alone.A postmortem found that every bone in her face had been broken as the result of a "sustained and brutal attack." The alarm was raised by a worried member of the public who called police.Robert Michael Sanders, 20, also known as Robert Tozer, spoke only to confirm his name and address in Hull. He stood between two security guards in the dock at the city's magistrates court, wearing a black T-shirt, as the charges were read.He was arrested on Sunday, and charged with murder late on Monday evening. He was remanded in custody by the magistrates and will appear at Hull crown court on Thursday.
