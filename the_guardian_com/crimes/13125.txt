A man has been arrested in connection with the disappearance of a Glasgow woman last seen four and a half years ago. Police say they have new information regarding Agnes Mechan, aged 66, known to friends and family as 'Nessa'.An unnamed 30-year-old man has been arrested and held in custody, and will appear at Glasgow Sheriff Court tomorrow. Meanwhile, a forensic search is underway at a property in Jamieson Street, Govanhill, Glasgow, close to where Mechan, a shopworker and credit agent aged in her sixties, was last seen.Mechan, a grandmother and mother-of-three, left her home around 7pm on 30 August 2002 to visit clients, telling her husband and children that she would be back in time to watch Coronation Street, but never returned.
