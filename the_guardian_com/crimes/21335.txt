Police are investigating after jewellery, an iPad and a phone were stolen from a dressing room at the Tower Ballroom in Blackpool that was being used by stars and dancers from the BBC's Strictly Come Dancing programme.It is believed that some of the celebrity contestants were victims of the crime, which was reported 45 minutes after the live show ended on Saturday night.A spokesman for Lancashire police said: "We are investigating a theft that has occurred at the Tower ballroom at Blackpool. It was reported to us at around 9:45pm that some items had been taken from a dressing room within the building.Police said it was not clear how the thieves had managed to gain entry to the room."Officers are investigating the theft and are making a number of inquiries to trace the person or people responsible and we would appeal to anyone who may have seen any suspicious activity in the area to contact police."
