Carole and Graham Fisher, murdered at their isolated Cornish home, were horrifically beaten before being shot several times, police said yesterday. Post-mortem examinations of the couple found they had undergone a prolonged ordeal. They were murdered at close range at the bungalow and petrol station they owned near Wadebridge, Cornwall, on Bonfire Night.A bus driver returning from the school run found Mrs Fisher's body outside the petrol station. Police found her 60-year-old husband shot dead inside the bungalow. The couple had owned the petrol station for 20 years. Police do not know the motive but several neighbours have said the couple kept a large amount of cash in their home.
