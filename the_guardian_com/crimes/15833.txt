A 16-year-old boy was stabbed to death in a fight involving a dozen teenagers in a subway, police said yesterday. Kashif Mahmood became embroiled in a fight with another teenager which escalated.During the melee in a subway in Ilford, Essex, he was stabbed and left for dead. He was found at 5pm yesterday and died later in King George's hospital in Ilford.All those involved in the fight were Asian youths of school or college age. Police have yet to establish whether Kashif, who lived in Ilford, was at school or college or working. Detectives are keeping an open mind on the motive for the stabbing but it does not appear to have been a random or racially-motivated attack. One 18-year-old man has been arrested and bailed.
