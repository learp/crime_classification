Five people have been arrested on suspicion of murder after a man's body was found on a beach on the Isle of Sheppey in Kent.Gary Pocock, 34, was found dead just before 6am on 7 August, when officers were called to Warden Bay Road, Leysdown, between Little Groves caravan site and the Isle of Sheppey Holiday Village, police said.Officers from the Kent and Essex serious crime directorate arrested a 19-year-old man from Warden, Sheppey, on Tuesday night.Two men, aged 20 and 44, both from Manor Park in east London, a 20-year-old man from Benfleet, Essex, and a 15-year-old boy from Warden were also arrested on Tuesday morning and remain in police custody.Two men aged 44 and 29 and a 43-year-old woman, who were arrested in connection with Pocock's death, have all been released without charge, a police spokesman said.A postmortem examination found that Pocock of Dagenham, east London, died of head injuries, he added.Anyone with information should call Kent police on 101 quoting CAD 07-0167, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
