Three drunken Asians who kicked a man to death were jailed for life at the Old Bailey yesterday and were told that they must serve a minimum of 15 years.Sajid Zulfiqar, Zahid Bashir and Imran Maqsood, all unemployed, from Ilford, north London, were later heard boasting that they had killed a white man, the court heard.But Judge Martin Stephens said the evidence did not show the attack on Christopher Yates was racially motivated because they had also assaulted a Pakistani waiter and a black man. The judge added: "These were random attacks."
