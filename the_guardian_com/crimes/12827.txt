A man appeared at Fenton magistrates court, Stoke-on-Trent, yesterday accused of murdering three children and a man who died in an explosion at their home.Mark Goldstraw, 31, is charged with the murder of Marcus Carter, seven, and his sisters Patricia, 10, and Samantha, 16, with their mother's partner, Roderick Hine, 44, in Cheddleton, near Leek, Staffordshire, between March 9 and 10. There was no application for bail and Mr Goldstraw was remanded in custody.The case was adjourned by district judge Graham Richards to Stafford crown court on Monday.
