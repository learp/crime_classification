A 59-year-old man died after a car was driven at him. A West Midlands police spokesman believed that the hit-and-run collision, which happened in Wolverhampton shortly after 5am yesterday, followed a confrontation between a group of men.The victim was taken to hospital suffering from severe injuries, but he was pronounced dead a short time after arriving there, said the spokesman.Police made a plea for witnesses to come forward to help them trace the vehicle's driver, who failed to stop at the scene in a cul-de-sac in the All Saints area. The spokesman said the victim's relatives had been informed and a formal identification and post-mortem examination would follow in due course.
