Three men have been arrested on suspicion of murder after a 21-year-old man died early on New Year's Day.The trio, aged 48, 31 and 29, were detained after the victim was found with a suspected stab wound outside a block of flats.Officers were called to a disturbance at the block in London Road, Sittingbourne, Kent, at 1.30am and the incident was resolved.But half an hour later police were called to the same place again following a report that a man was lying injured outside the flats with a suspected stab wound.The victim, from Sittingbourne, who has not been named, was taken to the Medway Maritime hospital where he was pronounced dead a short time later, a Kent police spokesman said.A postmortem examination was due to be carried out on Tuesday. Kent police appealed to anyone who heard or saw the disturbance to call them on 101 or Crimestoppers anonymously on 0800 555 111.
