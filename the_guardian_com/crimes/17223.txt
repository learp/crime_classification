Two people have been charged with assault after a father-of-six was stabbed while waiting to pick up his children outside school.Barry Parkes, 42, and Natalie Lawton, 30, were arrested following the stabbing of Steven Higginson, outside North Walkden primary school in Salford, Greater Manchester at about 3.30pm on Friday.Parkes, of Vestris Drive, Salford, was charged with wounding with intent. Lawton was charged with assault occasioning actual bodily harm.Both accused will appear at Manchester magistrates court on Monday.Higginson, 33, has since been discharged from hospital.
