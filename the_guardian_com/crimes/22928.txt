A mother-of-two was battered to death with a rock by a convicted criminal three days after he was released from a young offenders institution on licence, a court heard yesterday.Ben Redfern-Edwards, 21, of Disley, Cheshire, murdered beautician Jacqueline Ross, 44, to stop her reporting him to police after attacking her because he "dreaded" going back behind bars, a jury at Chester crown court heard.Mrs Ross, who was walking her dog at Disley, a beauty spot on the Cheshire border with Derbyshire, suffered "horrific" head injuries.The case continues.
