A teenage boy has been killed in a late-night street attack.Scotland Yard said officers were called to reports of a stabbing in Homerton High Street in Hackney, east London, shortly before midnight on Tuesday.“London ambulance service were called to the scene and a male, believed to be in his mid-teens, was found with injuries,” a spokeswoman said.Hackney police said the boy was 17.An air ambulance was called but the teenager was pronounced dead at the scene.No arrests have yet been made and road closures remain in place, police said.
