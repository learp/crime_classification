The Nottingham Forest defender Jamaal Lascelles has appeared in court over an alleged bar assault.Lascelles was at Derby crown court in connection with an incident earlier this year. The 21-year-old, who has played for England Under-21s, is accused of assault occasioning actual bodily harm at a Derby nightspot on 23 April.The player is on a season-long loan from Newcastle United, after joining the Premier League team from Forest in the summer.Lascelles, of Oakwood, Derby, was bailed to appear back at court on 11 March when he is due to enter a formal plea to the charge.Sarah Butler, prosecuting, said: “I am told this will be a definite trial,” indicating Lascelles intends to deny any wrongdoing.Judge Jonathan Gosling granted Lascelles conditional bail until his next court appearance, with a preliminary trial date set for the week of Monday 27 April.
