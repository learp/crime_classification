A man has died after being involved in a suspected robbery in north-west London.Ambulance officers found the man, aged in his thirties, suffering from a stab wound after being called to the junction of Parson and Brent streets in Hendon on Thursday afternoon.The victim was pronounced dead at the scene at 5.28pm. A postmortem will be carried out.Officers were understood to know the man's identity but were awaiting formal identification. Next of kin have not yet been told of his death.No arrests have been made and the homicide and major crime command will investigate the circumstances surrounding the man's death.
