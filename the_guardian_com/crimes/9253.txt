Jack Tweed has been charged with one count of rape, Scotland Yard said.The widower of the reality TV star Jade Goody will appear at Redbridge magistrates court in Ilford, Essex.Tweed, 22, from Chigwell in Essex, was arrested yesterday after a complaint from a young woman. Another man in his 20s has been bailed pending further inquiries, and a third man, aged 25, was arrested last night, the Metropolitan police said.The woman is said to have made the allegation after meeting the men at a central London club on Thursday night. It is alleged the attack took place at a home in Redbridge.Tweed's spokesman, Mark Thomas, has declined to comment on the arrest but confirmed that a lawyer had been hired for his client.Goody died in March this year, shortly after the couple were married.
