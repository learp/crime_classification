Robert Napper today pleaded not guilty to the 1992 murder of Rachel Nickell.Napper, formerly of south London, is accused of killing the model in front of her two-year-old son as they walked on Wimbledon common. He appeared by videolink for the 10-minute hearing at the Old Bailey.Nickell, 23, was stabbed 49 times and sexually assaulted in a frenzied attack on July 15 1992.Colin Stagg, from Roehampton, west London, was charged with her murder but cleared in 1994 after a judge threw out the case because police had used a "honey trap" to encourage him to confess.Napper was charged in November last year after a review of the case. The 41-year-old was today remanded in custody and the trial is scheduled to begin on November 11.
