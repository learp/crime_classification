The Newcastle United striker Andy Carroll has been charged with assault, following an incident at a city-centre nightclub.The 21-year-old is accused of attacking another reveller inside the Blu Bambu, on Newcastle's Bigg Market, in December. He will face magistrates in Newcastle later this month.Carroll has been a regular this season as Newcastle attempt to return to the Premier League after being relegated last season. The England Under-21 player has scored eight goals in 20 appearances.A Northumbria police spokesman said: "Andrew Carroll, 21, of Prestwick, Newcastle, has today been charged with assault. This follows an incident at Newcastle's Blu Bambu nightclub in the early hours of 7 December 2009."He will appear at Newcastle magistrates court on 25 February."
