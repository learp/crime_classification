A man has been charged with the murder of a teenager on Oxford Street, London, on Boxing Day.Thulani Khumalo, 20, will appear at Hendon magistrates court today charged with the killing of Seydou Diarrassouba, 18, from Mitcham, south-west London.A postmortem examination found Diarrassouba died from a single stab to the heart. He was knifed inside a Foot Locker store and collapsed outside in front of horrified shoppers near Bond Street underground station.Seven other youths arrested in connection with the investigation, including four teenagers, remain on police bail.
