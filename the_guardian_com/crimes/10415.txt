A murder inquiry has been launched after a mother and her four daughters died at their end-terrace home after a devastating fire which was started deliberately early yesterday.Caneze Riaz, 39, and her daughters Hannah, three, Alicia, 10, Sophia, 15, and Sayrah, 16, were killed in the blaze in Accrington, Lancashire, shortly before 2am. The girls' father, Mohammed Riaz, was rescued by fire crews and taken to Wythenshawe hospital, Manchester, where he is in a critical condition.The couple's 17-year-old son, Adam, only escaped the fire because he is in hospital with leukaemia.
