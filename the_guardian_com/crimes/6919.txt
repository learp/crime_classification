Police are looking for a woman on the run wearing a cow print onesie who reportedly robbed a man at gunpoint in Belfast city centre.PSNI (@PoliceServiceNI)Detectives are appealing for info following the report of an armed robbery that occurred in the Gt Victoria St area of Belfast this morningAugust 6, 2015Detectives are appealing for information from the public following the report of the armed robbery that occurred in the Great Victoria Street area of Belfast in Northern Ireland, during the early hours of Thursday morning.Police say a 20-year-old man was reportedly robbed by a woman wearing a distinctive cow print onesie around 3am, with a suspected firearm. She was in a blue Volkswagen Golf and was with two men at the time when they made off with a sum of cash.Detective Constable Gareth Hussey said: “I would ask anyone with any information about this incident to contact detectives.”
