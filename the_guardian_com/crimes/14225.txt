A man appeared in court today charged with the murder of a woman who was allegedly deliberately set on fire in a remote country lane.Anthony Clarke, 26, appeared at St Helen's magistrates court, accused of murdering Monika Szmecht, 20, who died on Monday after suffering severe burns to her entire body.After she was attacked, Ms Szmecht managed to stagger around 150 metres to a nearby cottage on Blindfoot Lane, Rainford, Merseyside, to seek help.She was taken to Whiston Hospital, but died later. Mr Clarke, of West Derby, Liverpool, was arrested shortly before midnight on Tuesday in the Croxteth area of Liverpool.During today's 15-minute hearing, he spoke only to confirm his name and personal details.No plea was entered. Mr Clarke was remanded in custody and will appear at Liverpool crown court on July 2.
