A teenager who caused the death of her friend when she crashed her car was jailed for 28 months . Gabriella Edmondson, 18, had pleaded guilty to causing the death of Grace Hadman by careless driving while over the alcohol limit. Oxford crown court heard that Grace, 17, a boarder at St Edward's school in Oxford, was a back-seat passenger in the Toyota Yaris when it left the A34 in Oxford and crashed in April. Edmondson, of Penn near High Wycombe, was 17 at the time and a fellow pupil at the public school. She escaped with minor injuries while a male passenger was seriously injured.
