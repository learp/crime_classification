A 51-year-old man has appeared in court charged with the attempted murder of a four-year-old boy who was found in the street with stab wounds.Steve Frogg, of Mansfield, Nottinghamshire, was remanded in custody after a brief hearing at the town's magistrates court.His alleged victim, who cannot be named for legal reasons, was found seriously injured in Mansfield on Monday afternoon. He is conscious and in a stable condition at the Queen's medical centre in Nottingham, police said.Frogg will next appear at Nottingham crown court on 25 April.
