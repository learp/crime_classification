Three brothers were charged last night with conspiring to murder a man who was shot as he delivered a takeaway. Safrajur Rahman Jahingir, 23, died from two gunshot wounds as he sat behind the wheel of his Vauxhall Corsa in Sheffield on Friday night. Last night South Yorkshire police said Mohim Khan, 36, Mukid Khan, 33, and Munaim Khan, 28, all from Sheffield, had been charged with conspiracy to murder. The shooting happened in the Parson Cross area of the city at 10.45pm. After the shots were fired, Mr Jahingir's car careered across Herries Road, narrowly missing a bus, and crashed into bushes.
