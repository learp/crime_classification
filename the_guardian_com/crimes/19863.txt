A man is in a critical condition after being shot by police who were called to a house where his 16-year-old daughter had suffered stab wounds.Firearms officers and police negotiators were sent to the house in Hersden, near Canterbury, Kent, at 11.20pm on Sunday.Sources said a girl of 16 inside the property had suffered stab wounds and her 40-year-old father was shot by a police officer. Police are not looking for anyone else in connection with the incident.Kent police said the pair were taken to hospital, where they remain in a critical condition.A police spokesman said the case had been voluntarily referred to the police watchdog, the Independent Police Complaints Commission.
