Four men have been charged over an alleged attempt to take control of computers at a bank branch to steal millions of pounds, Scotland Yard said.The charges come after an alleged incident at the branch of Santander bank in Surrey Quays, south-east London.The four men were charged with conspiracy to steal and will appear at Westminster magistrates court on Saturday.Eight other people were bailed to a date in mid-November pending further inquiries, police said.Police carried out searches at addresses in Westminster, Hounslow, Hillingdon, Brent and Richmond, and also in Slough, where property was seized.
