A 15-year-old girl and a 41-year-old man are being questioned on suspicion of murdering a man and a woman, police said.The teenager and the man were arrested today after two people were found dead at separate addresses in Ipswich, Suffolk, a police spokeswoman said.The woman was found dead at about 11am on Sunday and the man was found dead at about 4am today, the spokeswoman added. Police said the two suspects were in custody and being questioned by detectives.
