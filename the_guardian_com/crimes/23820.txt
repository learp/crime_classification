Police yesterday arrested a man on suspicion of causing a large fire which destroyed more than 100 hectares (250 acres) of heathland.About 100 residents were evacuated as the fire spread at Canford Heath, Poole, Dorset, on Sunday night. Some 170 firefighters fought for three hours to extinguish the blaze.A police spokesman said a 37-year-old man from Poole had been arrested and was helping with their inquiries.It is estimated it could take 15 years for the heathland to grow back. No one was injured and residents were allowed to return to their homes later that evening.
