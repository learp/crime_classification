The former partner of Shannon Matthews' mother will go on trial in September on charges of possessing child pornography, a district judge said today.Craig Meehan, 22, appeared at Dewsbury magistrates court accused of possessing 140 indecent images of children, which were allegedly found on a computer at the home he shared with Karen Matthews.Nine-year-old Shannon went missing on February 19 and was discovered hidden in the base of a bed in a flat just one mile from her Dewsbury Moor home after a 24-day search by police.District Judge Jonathan Bennett said Meehan would be remanded in custody until the beginning of his five-day trial on September 8.No application for bail was made.
