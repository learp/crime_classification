Four men were tonight charged with the murder of a man found dying in the back of a van in Sunningdale, Berkshire, on Wednesday. Robert Johnston, 56, and his sons Tom Johnston, 24, and Ben Johnston, 26, all from Pampisford, Cambridge, and Shaun Matthews, 54, from Whittlesford, Cambridge, were charged with killing Shaleem Amar, 33, from Hancocks Mount, Sunningdale. Amar was discovered after Thames Valley Police firearms officers pulled over the vehicle on the A30. He died from head injuries. The four are in custody, due to appear at Slough magistrates' court.
