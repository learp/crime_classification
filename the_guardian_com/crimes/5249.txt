Police have appealed for witnesses after a woman was assaulted on a rush-hour train.The 40-year-old needed treatment for a head injury at Brixton railway station in south London after the attack.Rush-hour services run by the Southeastern train company were delayed after the incident, with ambulance staff, Metropolitan police and British transport police attending the scene.Officers from British transport police have launched an investigation into the incident, and are urging witnesses to contact them on 0800 405040.
