Two people are in police custody today in connection with the death of a toddler.Cheshire police said a 22-year-old female and a 24-year-old male, both from Runcorn, were helping with inquiries.A police spokesman said the two-year-old boy, also from Runcorn, died last night.A postmortem examination will be carried out later today to establish the cause of death.Ambulance crews discovered the child at an address at 10.30pm yesterday.
