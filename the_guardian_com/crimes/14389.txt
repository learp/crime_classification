A 13-year-old boy died today from head injuries he suffered in a fight near West Ham's football ground in east London.Scotland Yard launched a murder inquiry following the death.Five youths are in custody in connection with the fight, which broke out close to Upton Park on Wednesday afternoon.Police said the victim was with a group of six to 10 youths who were thought to have been fighting a larger gang.Officers are doing forensic tests on items taken from the scene, but it was not known if a weapon was used to attack the boy, who was originally from Lithuania.
