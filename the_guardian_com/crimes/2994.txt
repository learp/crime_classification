A teenager is in a serious condition in hospital after being shot in the back in west London in the early hours of Sunday morning.Police were called to an incident in Westbourne Park, west London, at 12.35am. They found a 16-year-old boy with a gunshot wound at the junction of St Luke's Road and Tavistock Crescent with a gunshot wound.Scotland Yard said officers from the Trident shootings team, which investigates gun crime in the black community, were appealing for help from witnesses or anyone who may have information about the incident. They are asked to call the Trident team on 020 8785 8580 or Crimestoppers on 0800 555111 to remain anonymous.
