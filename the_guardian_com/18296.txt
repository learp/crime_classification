The jury in the Damilola Taylor murder trial will retire on Monday to consider its verdicts, the judge said today.Mr Justice Hooper told the four women and seven men that he would complete his summing up with concluding remarks on Monday morning. Damilola, 10, bled to death from a thigh wound caused by the broken neck of a bottle on the North Peckham estate, south London, on November 27 2000. Two brothers aged 16 deny alternative charges of murder and manslaughter, and assault with intent to rob.The trial continues.
