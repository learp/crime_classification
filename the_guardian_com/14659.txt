A 46-year-old man accused of running off with a 14-year-old schoolgirl today appeared in court charged with abduction and other offences.David Milner, of Victoria Street, Gillingham, Kent, faced one charge of abduction and a total of eight other offences when he appeared before Medway magistrates court in Kent. Mr Milner, who was wearing a dark grey shirt and glasses, spoke only to give his name, address and date of birth during the short hearing. There was no application for bail and he was remanded in custody until September 9.Mr Milner, who is divorced, was detained on Monday morning following the disappearance of the girl from her home last Thursday. The pair were found by police in Scotland on Monday and the girl was reunited with her mother.
