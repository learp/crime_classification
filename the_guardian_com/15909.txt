Four British men have appeared in court in Cyprus over the death of a teenage motorcyclist, officials confirmed yesterday.Julian Harrington, 38, Luke Atkinson, 22, Michael Binnington, 21, and James Goodwin, 21, all from Essex, were detained after a crash involving their rented car. Christos Papiris, 17, died after the collision between Protaras and Ayia Napa on August 18.The men were remanded in custody for eight days on charges of murder, conspiracy to kill and attempted murder. Cyprus police have said the crash followed a fight outside a nightclub.
