A hospital consultant whose gross negligence led to the death of a mother of two has been jailed for two years.Sudhanshu Garg missed vital opportunities to properly investigate a kidney infection that was developing into a life-threatening infection and blockage.Leeds crown court heard how Lisa Quinn, 37, died at Bradford royal infirmary on Monday 25 August 2008, three days after she went into Accident and Emergency.Quinn, who left children aged 10 and 12, was admitted to hospital on a Friday and her condition deteriorated over the bank holiday weekend.She was not given a crucial ultrasound scan until two days after arriving at the hospital.
