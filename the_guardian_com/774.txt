A postmortem examination confirmed yesterday that the body of a baby found on wasteland in Smethwick, Birmingham, was that of six-month-old Troy Simpson, who went missing from his home 300 yards away three days ago.The disappearance of the child, who lived with his mother and grandmother, triggered a huge search in the area before the body was found by a small stream. West Midlands police have been given extra time by magistrates to question two men and two women. Police sealed off the area where the body was found for examination.
