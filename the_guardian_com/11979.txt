One man has died and two have been injured after a mass brawl at the shopping centre next to the Olympic Park.Police were called at 4.24pm to reports of a fight involving a large group of males in "The Street" on the ground floor of Westfield shopping centre in Stratford, east London.A man, believed to be in his 20s, suffered stab wounds and was pronounced dead at the scene.The shopping centre will be the gateway to the Olympics for many visitors to London this summer.A police spokesman said they were awaiting a formal identification of the victim and were establishing the full circumstances of the incident. One man has been arrested.
