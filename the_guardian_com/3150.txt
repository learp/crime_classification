A teenage boy has been given four years' youth detention after pleading guilty to raping four girls aged between seven and 10.The boy, aged 14 at the time of the offences, was sentenced at Manchester crown court yesterday.The victims were playing in Mandley park, Salford, in July last year when they were approached by the teenager, who cannot be named for legal reasons. He led the girls into a secluded area and suggested they play a game of dare.A 10-year-old girl who refused to be involved in the game ran back to the park and asked some adults for help, but none was offered, the court heard.
