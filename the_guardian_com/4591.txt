A Christmas Eve raid by armed officers at a warehouse in Upper Siddington, near Cirencester, thwarted plans for a robbery, police have said.The intelligence-led operation, which involved Gloucestershire police, Metropolitan police flying squad officers, a helicopter and dog teams, arrived at the Gloucestershire industrial estate at around 8.50pm on Christmas Eve. Five men were arrested at gunpoint on suspicion of conspiracy to rob and taken to north London for further questioning. Police suspect the group was plotting a robbery in the region during the Christmas break.
