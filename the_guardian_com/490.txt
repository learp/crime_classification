A man was arrested last night following the murder of a teenager in a chalet at one of Butlins' busiest holiday camps.Police called to the complex at Minehead, Somerset, early yesterday following reports of a disturbance, found the body of a 19-year-old woman. The victim, believed to be from Mid-Glamorgan, South Wales, died in a nearby hospital.A 26-year-old man, believed to be her boyfriend and also from Mid-Glamorgan, has been arrested on suspicion of murder and last night was in custody at Taunton police station.Around 4,500 holidaymakers were staying at the time of the killing. The victim, thought to be from Hengoed, near Caerphilly, has still to be formally identified. The results of a post mortem examination are expected today.
