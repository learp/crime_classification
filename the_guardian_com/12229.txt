Three people have been charged in connection with the death of six-month-old Troy Simpson, who was reported missing last Tuesday, sparking a high-profile police search. The child's body was found 300 metres from his home in Smethwick, Birmingham, the following day.Last week, magistrates granted West Midlands police a further 36 hours to question three people arrested on the day the baby went missing. Police said two women, aged 17 and 47, and a 20-year-old man were charged with perverting the course of justice. All three will appear at Birmingham magistrates court today.
