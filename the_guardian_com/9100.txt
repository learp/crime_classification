Police have charged two teenagers over the murder of a teenager shot dead in the stairwell of a south London housing estate.Sylvester Akapalara, 17, was killed on the fourth floor of Heron House on the Pelican Estate in Peckham, south-east London, on Wednesday. He had been shot in the head and chest.Scotland Yard said today that Sodiq Adeojo, 18, of Shurlands Gardens, Peckham, and a 17-year-old male youth had been charged over the murder.Both will appear before Camberwell Green magistrates court on Monday.Akapalara, a promising athlete, was found bleeding to death on the stairwell of the tower block shortly after 4.30pm on Wednesday. Paramedics were called, but he was pronounced dead at the scene.
