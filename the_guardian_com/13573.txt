A man has been charged with murdering his identical twin brother in a fight on New Year's Eve. Christopher Cerqua, 31, suffered serious injuries in the incident at his Hampshire home on the evening of 31 December.Police were called to the house in Hythe at 9pm and Mr Cerqua was rushed to Southampton general hospital, where he died later that night. His twin, Robert Cerqua, who lived at the same address, was arrested in the early hours of January 1 and was charged with murder tonight, Hampshire police said. He will appear at Southampton magistrates court tomorrow. The cause of Mr Cerqua's death is yet to be released.Detectives from the Hampshire major investigation team are leading the murder inquiry.
