An 18-year-old man was today remanded in custody charged with the murder of schoolfriends Steven Bayliss and Nuttawut Nadauld in Berkshire.Thomas Palmer, of Blagrove Drive, Wokingham, Berkshire, appeared before magistrates in Reading charged with killing the teenagers. Steven, 16, and his 14-year-old neighbour Nuttawut - known as Twood - were found dead on a quiet wooded lane at the edge of Wokingham on Sunday night. Mr Palmer spoke only to give his name and date of birth to the court during the four-minute hearing. Royston Hine, the chairman of the three-strong panel of magistrates, told him he would be remanded in custody to appear before Reading crown court for a preliminary hearing on October 3. No application for bail was made.
