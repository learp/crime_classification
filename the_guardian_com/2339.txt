The mother of murdered black teenager Anthony Walker yesterday said she would be prepared to meet her son's killers one day.Gee Walker's 18-year-old son was killed with an ice axe in a racist attack in Huyton, Merseyside, last July.Paul Taylor, 20, and Michael Barton ,17, were convicted of the murder in November and jailed for life.Mrs Walker told BBC Radio Merseyside she hoped that in time she would be able to meet them and ask why they killed him.She said: "It won't be easy. I am not ready, to be honest; the pain is still raw. Like I have said before, forgiveness takes time."
