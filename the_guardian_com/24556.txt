Police have arrested four people on suspicion of murder after a 50-year-old man who had been asked to help after a fight at a party was stabbed in the street.Officers were called to Brooklyn Road, Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire, at 12.50am on Saturday morning by a gang.Police arrested a 19-year-old man and two women aged 19 and 20. Another 19-year-old man has been in custody since Saturday afternoon.A spokesman said all four remain in custody on suspicion of murder.Detectives investigating the incident have been granted extra time to question the 20-year-old woman.The dead man was Peter Brocklesby, from the Cleckheaton area.A post-mortem examination revealed the cause of his death was stab wounds.Detective Chief Inspector Simon Bottomley said police were keen to speak to anyone who saw the stabbing or the earlier fight.
