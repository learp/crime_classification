Three people were arrested today following an incident at the T in the Park music festival in which two men were seriously hurt.Two 20-year-olds from Fife were found with serious injuries on a walkway at around 11.50pm on Sunday.Both are thought to have been stabbed and are said to be in a serious but stable condition in hospital at Dundee. Officers investigating the incident, which is being treated as attempted murder, said three men in their 20s had been arrested.Police are also investigating a sexual assault of a woman in her 20s at the festival, near the bus parking area on Saturday night.
