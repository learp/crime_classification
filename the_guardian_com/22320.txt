A 32-year-old man was shot in the chest on his doorstep at the weekend during an attack by two masked gunmen in Sheffield, police said yesterday.Detectives said one of the men fired a shotgun at the victim while the other brandished a handgun. The injured man staggered to a neighbour's house, where an ambulance was called. He suffered serious injuries and is being treated in hospital following the incident in the Parson Cross area of Sheffield, South Yorkshire, at around 9pm on Saturday.Police said they had yet to establish a motive for what they believed was a targeted attack.
