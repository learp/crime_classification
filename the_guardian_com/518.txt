Police were questioning a man on suspicion of murder last night after a person was pushed in front of a Tube train during rush hour.The male victim died after being pushed from the northbound platform of the Victoria Line at Highbury and Islington station in north London at about 6.15pm on Friday. Officers are appealing for any witnesses who were on the platform at the time of the incident to contact them.A British Transport Police spokesman said last night that the deceased had yet to be formally identified. The Victoria line was closed between Warren Street and Walthamstow Central following the incident, and is expected to reopen tomorrow morning.
