A man has been charged with the murder of a sex worker in London.The body of Maria Duque-Tunjano, 48, a Briton originally from Colombia, was discovered at a flat in Earls Court, west London, on Friday.A Metropolitan police spokesman said Robert Richard Fraser, 39, was charged on Wednesday night with her murder.A postmortem examination found the cause of death was a blunt trauma to the head.Fraser, of no fixed address, has also been charged with two counts of robbery, false imprisonment and sexual assault.He was remanded in custody to appear at Westminster magistrates court on Thursday morning.
