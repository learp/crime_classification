Police have launched a murder investigation after a decomposing body was found in a binliner on wasteland close to the M62 in East Yorkshire.A dog walker made the discovery near junction 35 in East Cowick near Goole on Wednesday night.Humberside police said the unidentified man's body was clothed but it is not yet know how old he was, how he died nor how long the body had been there.A police spokesman said: "We don't know how long he has been dead but the body appears to have been in that position for some time."
