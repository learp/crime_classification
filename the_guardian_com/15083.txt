Police arrested two men and a woman yesterday in their investigation into the murder of student Anthony Walker, 18, who was killed with an axe in a park in Knowsley, Merseyside, in July.The men, aged 31 and 18, and the 34-year-old woman, all from Liverpool, were arrested on suspicion of perverting the course of justice. A police spokeswoman said the three arrested yesterday were being questioned at a local police station. Two men, Paul Taylor, 20, and Michael Barton, 17, have been charged with Mr Walker's murder. Twelve people have now been arrested over the murder.
