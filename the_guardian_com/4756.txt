A teenage babysitter beat a two-year-old girl to death, a court heard. The 15-year-old boy inflicted 68 injuries on Demi Leigh Mahon on 15 July last year, Manchester crown court was told. The boy, who cannot be named for legal reasons, denies murder. Her mother, Ann Marie McDonald, said she had popped out to pick up her child benefit. She said when she returned to the house in Eccles, Salford, 90 minutes later he told her: "I picked her up – I might have hurt her – and she fell in the park." Howard Bentham QC, prosecuting, said the defendant inflicted 68 injuries and a bite mark on the child. The trial continues.
