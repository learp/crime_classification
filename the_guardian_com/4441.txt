Four brothers and a friend who ran a family empire trafficking eastern European women into Britain for prostitution were jailed yesterday. The Albanian gang forced their victims to work round the clock in seven west London vice dens.Brothers Flamur, Agron and Bedari Demarku, and friend Izzet Fejzullahu, were convicted of trafficking and prostitution at Southwark crown court last month. A fourth brother, Xhevair, 29, pleaded guilty before the trial.Flamur, 34, and Agron, 21, got 18 years each. Izzet Fejzullahu, 32, got 14 years, Bedari, 22, eight and Xhevair, 29, five.
