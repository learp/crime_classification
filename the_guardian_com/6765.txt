Police are searching for a man who is suspected of stabbing a nine-year-old boy at a skatepark on Monday.The child was said to be in a stable condition after being transported by air ambulance to Leeds general infirmary with chest and arm injuries.The incident occurred at about 4.40pm at the Carnegie skatepark in the town of Shipley, north of Bradford.West Yorkshire police described a suspect in the stabbing as white, aged in his 20s and wearing a cap and glasses. He is believed to have been carrying a walking stick.Police said it was being treated as a critical incident and numerous resources have been deployed to the scene.Anyone with information have been asked to telephone 101 or Crimestoppers in confidence on 0800 555 111.
