A mother appeared in court today charged with the murder of her two children after a fire at their family home.Fiona Adams, 23, was charged last night with the murder of Niamh, five, and Cayden, two, who died in the fire at the house in Buxton, Derbyshire, last month.Adams escaped from the house by jumping from an upstairs window just before midnight on 23 April.She appeared at Chesterfield magistrates court charged with two counts of murder, arson with intent to endanger life, and arson being reckless as to whether life was endangered.With one leg in plaster and using crutches due to injuries suffered in the fire, Adams's voice broke as she confirmed her name and address. She shook her head as the charges were read out to the court.No application for bail was made and Adams was remanded in custody to appear at Nottingham crown court on Monday.
