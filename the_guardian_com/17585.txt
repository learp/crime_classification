Four people, including a 15-year-old boy and girl, appeared at Leicester magistrates' court today charged with the murder of teenager Bradley Whitfield on New Year's Day.Bradley, 16, was found with a fatal puncture wound to the neck in Fosse Road South, in Leicester, in the early hours of January 1 after he celebrated the new year with friends.Darren Carr, 34, and his partner Nicola Faulds, 31, both of Braunstone, were remanded in custody today accused of his murder.The teenage co-accused, who cannot be named for legal reasons, were granted conditional bail.A preliminary hearing has been set for January 18 at Leicester cown court.A postmortem examination revealed that the student died after suffering a 4in-deep (10cm) neck wound, which severed a main artery.
