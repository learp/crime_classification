Three youths have been charged with the manslaughter of a 13-year-old boy who died in a street fight.Deividas Strizegauskas suffered a severe head injury in the clash in Newham, east London, last Wednesday. Scotland Yard said the victim, who lived in the area, was with a group of six to 10 youths who were thought to have been fighting with a larger gang. Two 15-year-olds and a 14-year-old, all from east London, will appear before Stratford magistrates today.A fourth youth is still being questioned and a fifth has been bailed pending further inquiries. A sixth was released without charge.
