Two boys aged 14 and 15 were arrested today in connection with the double shooting of two teenagers, police said.The victims, aged 18 and 19, are in a "serious but stable" condition after being shot in Neasden, north-west London, on Saturday night.Detectives made two arrests at two different addresses in Brent this morning, Scotland Yard said. The suspects are in custody at a west London police station.Officers and the London Ambulance Service were called to the scene of the shooting at Blackbird Hill, Neasden, where they found two young men with gunshot wounds. The victims were taken to separate hospitals.A Metropolitan police spokeswoman said: "Officers from the Met service shootings team are investigating."
