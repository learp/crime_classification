A policeman has been arrested for allegedly filming up women's skirts in Trafalgar Square, it was reported yesterday.Scotland Yard confirmed a 44-year-old Metropolitan Police officer had been detained in central London on suspicion of causing a public nuisance. He allegedly claimed he was on an anti-terror surveillance operation, the Daily Mirror reported. A Scotland Yard spokeswoman said the man had been bailed until September pending further inquiries. She confirmed he was involved in specialist operations but could not say if he had been suspended from duties.
