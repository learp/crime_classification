A 52-year-old man was today arrested in connection with the disappearance of missing schoolgirl Amanda Dowler, Surrey police said.He is being questioned this evening at Surrey police station.Amanda, known as Milly, was last seen on 21 March. She went missing while walking home from the train station in Walton-on-Thames, about three miles from Chertsey.Last month police arrested a 36-year-old man in connection Milly's disappearance. He was later released on bail.
