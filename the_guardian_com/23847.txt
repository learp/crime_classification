A 38-year-old man appeared in court today charged with the manslaughter of a young woman who died on a railway track.Darren Palmer, of Clacton, Essex, did not enter a plea during the short hearing at Colchester magistrates court. He was remanded in custody and will appear at Chelmsford crown court in June.Kelly Mack, 29, of Clacton, was hit by a train at a level crossing in Colchester on March 27. It was reported at the time that Mack's foot became trapped in wooden slats as she crossed the line.
