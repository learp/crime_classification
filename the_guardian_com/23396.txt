A male believed to be in his late teens was stabbed to death in south west London tonight, Scotland Yard said.The incident happened in Guildford Road, south Lambeth.A Yard spokesman said officers were called there at 7.05pm.They were responding to reports of an assault in progress and found the victim suffering from one or more stab wounds to the stomach.He was taken to hospital but pronounced dead at 8.15pm.If the victim was indeed a teenager, he would be the 21st to die violently in the capital so far this year.
