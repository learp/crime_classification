A gang of five men armed with baseball bats is being sought by police after a 29-year-old man died of head injuries following a fight.Serioza Lawskoski, from Leacroft Road in the Peartree area of Derby, was found unconscious in the street at 7.40pm on Saturday evening.He was treated at the scene by paramedics and taken to the Royal Derby Hospital but was later pronounced dead.The victim was discovered on Cummings Street. "He had been attacked by a group of about five masked men who were wearing dark clothing, heavy jackets and balaclavas," said Derby police. "They were armed with baseball bats or sticks."They left the area along Lower Dale Road and up Silverhill Road. A murder investigation has begun and a post mortem examination will be carried out later. Teams of forensic experts are examining the scene and extra uniformed patrols will be in the area for the foreseeable future."Detective Superintendent Andy Stokes who is leading the investigation, said: "This is a tragic incident and I'd like to offer my condolences to the victim's family and friends."A post-mortem examination revealed that Lawskoski died of head injuries. A 25-year-old man from Derby has been arrested on suspicion of murder and is being questioned by police, a spokeswoman added.Officers are appealing for anyone who was in the area between 7pm and 8pm on Saturday to contact them on 0345-123 3333.
