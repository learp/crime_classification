The University of London has suspended Brian Dodgeon, a member of its research staff, following the death of 15-year-old Isobel Reilly at a party at his west London home in the early hours of Saturday. Dodgeon, 60, a lecturer at the university's Institute of Education, has been suspended on full pay with immediate effect.Dodgeon was held by police on suspicion of child abandonment and possessing drugs following the death of the teenager, who had taken a cocktail of illegal substances including ecstasy.The academic, whose research subjects have included health inequalities and alcohol consumption patterns, was bailed to a date in June.A post-mortem examination will be carried out on Wednesday.
