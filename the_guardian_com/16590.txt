A daylight firebomb attack on a convenience store in south London has claimed a second victim.Hamidi Hamidullah, 31, died in hospital yesterday following the arson at the Pricecutter shop in Clapham Road, Kennington, last Thursday. His fellow worker, Khizar Hayat, burnt to death when he was trapped in the store.Police said they believed the attack was the work of a racist serial arsonist. On Monday they arrested a 33-year-old man in nearby Stockwell. He is still being questioned over the attack and two similar bombs that were thrown into Asian shops in the past three weeks.
