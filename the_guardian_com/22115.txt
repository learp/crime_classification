Fears were growing yesterday for the safety of a businessman abducted at gunpoint from his home in Pollokshields, Glasgow.Javed Mukhtar, who runs newsagent stores, was seized on Friday by three masked men. A shot was fired but no one was injured. Strathclyde police expressed concern about the health of the 58-year-old. He is diabetic and has been fasting in observance of Ramadan. No motive had been established. Mr Mukhtar's son Rizwad said: "Neither my mother, my sisters or my brothers have slept at all since this happened. We just want our dad back safe and well."
