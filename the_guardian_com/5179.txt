A former police constable who was stabbed to death outside his home in a Welsh village was about to go on trial for possessing more than 2,000 child pornography images.Geoffrey Harries, 49, died from his injuries in hospital after being stabbed in the street in Dythel Park, Trimsaran, Dyfed, on Saturday morning.After his death it emerged Harries was a former Dyfed Powys police officer who had resigned from the force in March after being charged in connection with child pornography.He was released on bail after appearing in Cardiff magistrates court on May 13 charged with 15 offences of making indecent images of children and one of possessing indecent images. His trial was due to start next Monday.A 30-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the incident, and remains in custody.
