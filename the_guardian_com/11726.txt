An 85-year-old woman is in hospital after being beaten by a man to whom she opened her front door between 6.30pm on Christmas Eve and 9.30am on Christmas Day.Detectives think the attack was sexually motivated, and are warning other residents in Pitsea, Essex, to be on their guard when opening the door to strangers. They said the woman was extremely traumatised and had so far been unable to give them much information.Her injuries are not thought to be life-threatening. Officers hunting her assailant will talk to her again over the next few days.
