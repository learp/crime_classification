A man appeared in court accused of murdering a 16-year-old boy in south London in the early hours of last Thursday.David Taitt, 20, appeared at Camberwell Green magistrates' court charged with the murder of Abukar Mahamed, who was killed after being chased by a gang on bikes into the Stockwell Gardens estate.Taitt, who lives on the estate, spoke only to confirm his name during the brief hearing at which he was told his case was being sent to the Old Bailey for a preliminary hearing on 1 November. He was remanded into custody.A post mortem examination showed Abukar, who lived on the Tulse Hill estate in south London, was killed by a single gunshot wound to the neck.
