A man appeared in court yesterday charged with the murder of Oxford academic Barbara Johnston.Michael Humphries, 42, of Faringdon, Oxfordshire, spoke only to confirm his name and address at the hearing before Oxford magistrates. Mr Humphries is accused of stabbing 55-year-old Dr Johnston 49 times and strangling her with her own jumper between January 23 and 26 this year.An application for bail by Mr Humphries's solicitor Lee Reed was rejected. The case was transferred to Oxford crown court for a preliminary hearing on February 6.
