Two 18-year-old women have been arrested following the fatal stabbing of a 17-year-old girl last night.Police were called to a disturbance in a residential street in Croydon, south London, where the victim was found suffering from a stab wound.The girl was taken to Mayday University hospital and was pronounced dead at 8.43pm. Police have not yet named the victim.The Metropolitan police confirmed that the two women were arrested at the scene and were being held in custody at separate south London police stations.A spokesman said: "They were known to the victim, but we cannot comment on the nature of their relationship to her."
