Detectives could speak to the main suspect in the murder of a Harrow schoolmaster's daughter by the end of the week, it emerged yesterday.William Jaggs, 22, was arrested on suspicion of murder after Lucy Braham, 25, a fashion graduate, was stabbed to death in her home at Harrow-on-the-Hill, north-west London. Jaggs, an Oxford undergraduate, was taken to hospital, having apparently stabbed himself.His condition is described as conscious and stable. Police are likely to wait until he is discharged to interview him, and it is understood that could be this week or early next week.
