A man was killed and another left critically injured after a double shooting on a street in Liverpool in the early hours of Thursday morning, Merseyside police say.Emergency services were called at about 1am after reports of shots being fired on Ravensthorpe Green, a residential street bordering parkland in the Norris Green area of the city.When police arrived they found two men who had been shot. Paramedics declared one man, believed to be in his 30s, dead at the scene. The other, thought to be in his 20s, was taken to hospital where his condition is described as critical.Officers have cordoned off the scene and are making house-to-house inquiries. Extra police patrols have been put on in the area.A Merseyside police statement said: "The motive for the shooting is not yet clear and officers are appealing for anyone with information to come forward. An investigation to establish the full circumstances of the incident is under way."Anyone with information is asked to call the force's Matrix team, which specialises in gun crime, on 0151 777 5699, or Crimestoppers on 0800 555 111.
