A man has been charged with the murder of a teenage girl who was found stabbed to death in a car boot.The man will appear before magistrates in Llanelli, Wales, tomorrow.The body of Chanelle Sasha Jones, known to her friends as Sasha, was discovered in a Ford Fiesta in Aberaeron, in Ceredigion, west Wales, on Sunday.The 17-year-old, from Cardigan, had been reported missing by a family member earlier that day. The car was stopped by Dyfed Powys police and a man was arrested at the scene. The Independent Police Complaints Commission is investigating whether officers dealt with the missing person report adequately.
