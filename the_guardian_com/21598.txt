A man charged with the murder of a woman who was run over as she attempted to stop her sports car from being stolen admitted causing death by dangerous driving when he appeared in court today.Lynda Hankey, 42, died of head injuries after being thrown into the air over the yellow MG sports car outside her home in Worsley Mesnes, in Wigan, in January. She died in hospital several hours later.Shaun Higgins, of Church Street, Leigh, denied murder when he appeared at Liverpool crown court.However, the 23-year-old admitted aggravated vehicle-taking and causing death by dangerous driving while disqualified.He was remanded in custody until 5 July, when he will stand trial on the murder charge.
