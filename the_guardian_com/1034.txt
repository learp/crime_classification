A convicted rapist who allegedly abducted a teenage girl from a busy street, assaulted her and held her captive for 15 hours was last night arrested by police. Alan Grant, 49, from Stockport, Greater Manchester, was arrested in the north Derbyshire area near the border with Manchester. He allegedly snatched the girl from a street in Wrexham, north Wales, on Friday.Two other people, a man and a woman, were also arrested yesterday. The man, who has not been named, has been charged with perverting the course of justice and the woman is being questioned on suspicion of assisting an offender. The 15-year-old girl was forcibly taken off a street in Wrexham on Friday afternoon and assaulted before she was released the next morning 70 miles away in Bangor.
