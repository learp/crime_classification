A man appeared in court today charged with the rape and murder of a two-year-old girl.Michael Mullen, 21, was arrested after the toddler was found in a pool of blood at her home in Gipton, Leeds, on Sunday night. At this morning's hearing the judge imposed an order banning the identification of the dead girl.Mr Mullen was remanded in custody to appear at Leeds crown court on February 21.During the brief hearing at Leeds magistrates court, Mr Mullen spoke only to confirm his name, age, address and that he understood the charges.
