A teenage girl has been charged with the murder of 16-year-old Julie Sheriff, who was left in a coma for more than four months before dying on 21 September, after an attack in Battersea, London, when she sustained head injuries, Scotland Yard said .The accused 15-year-old girl, of no fixed address, was first charged with grievous bodily harm and appeared in court on 10 May, three days after the attack.While the teenager was on remand for that allegation, officers decided to treat the case as murder. She is now held in secure accommodation and will appear at Balham youth court on 18 October.Julie was visiting friends when she was attacked in Falcon Road, Battersea.
