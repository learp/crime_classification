A teenager who was stabbed to death in London on Saturday has been named as Carl Beatson Asiedu, 19, from Norbury, south London. He was found by police in one of three cars stopped after running a red light at 5.12am. An ambulance was called but he was pronounced dead at the scene. Police believe he was stabbed in a fight outside a nightclub in Kennington. At around the same time he was discovered, a second man – also believed to be 19 years old – was found near the club with stab wounds. He was taken to hospital and has since been discharged. Police have arrested two men, aged 18 and 20.
