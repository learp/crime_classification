Police are hunting a man who stabbed to death a bus passenger. Richard Whelan, 28, from Kentish Town, north London, who was travelling with his girlfriend on Friday night, died in hospital after being stabbed several times.Police said the suspect boarded the No 43 bus in Upper Street, Islington, at 9.45pm. He began throwing takeaway food at passengers on the top deck and a row broke out with Mr Whelan.The attacker got off the bus at Holloway Road and fled towards an Esso garage. He is described as black, in his 20s, just under 6ft tall, with afro-style hair. Police want to speak with passengers on the top deck of a bus following the 43.
