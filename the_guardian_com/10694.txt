A man who handed himself in after nearly a month on the run has been charged with the murder of his ex-girlfriend.Michael Cope, 28, was arrested on Friday night after police were called to an address in Leigh, Greater Manchester.Linzi Ashton, 25, a mother of two, was discovered with multiple injuries at her home in Winton, Salford, on 29 June.She had made separate complaints to Greater Manchester police in April and May that Cope had raped and beaten her.Greater Manchester police said Cope, of no fixed address, was also charged with separate offences of rape and assault.
