Two adults and three youths were arrested yesterday on suspicion of the murders of an elderly couple at their home.Police used search warrants to raid five houses in Faversham, Kent, in the hunt for the killers of Terry Martin, 72, and his wife, Vera, 78. The five were being questioned at separate police stations in Kent.Detectives also said that the couple, yet to be officially named, died from multiple injuries. Police believe the killings may have resulted from a bungled robbery after the couple's Daihatsu car was found burned out a few miles away.
