A man and woman have been remanded in custody after appearing in court charged with the murder of a 15-year-old boy who was found dead in a bath on Christmas Day, police said today .Eric Baikubi, 27, of Newham, east London, and Magalie Bamu, also 27, appeared before Thames magistrates in London.Officers discovered the badly-injured teenager at a flat in Newham at around lunchtime on Christmas Day.A post-mortem examination at East Ham mortuary failed to determine the exact cause of death, and police are awaiting the results of further tests.A Met police spokesman said the pair were remanded in custody following a short hearing. They will appear at the Old Bailey in London on Thursday.
