Former nightclub bouncer Levi Bellfield, 37, was charged yesterday with the murder of French student Amelie Delagrange, attempted murder of Kate Sheedy and Irma Dragoshi, and attempted abduction and false imprisonment of Anne Maria Rennie.Mr Bellfield, formerly of West Drayton, west London, is due to appear at Horseferry magistrates court in London today.Miss Delagrange, 22, was found battered to death in August 2004 in Twickenham, south-west London. Miss Sheedy, 18, was run over twice by a car after she got off a bus in Isleworth, west London in May 2004.
