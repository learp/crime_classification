Police are investigating the murder of an 18-year-old model who was stabbed near her home in south London early on Sunday. Sally Anne Bowman (right) was attacked as she returned to her home in Croydon after a night out with friends.A postmortem found multiple stab wounds. Detectives appealed yesterday for anyone who was in the area and saw or heard anything between 4.30am and 6.30am to get in touch with them. Three men - aged 20, 25 and 26 - were arrested yesterday morning. The two older men were later bailed to return to a London police station on October 12.
