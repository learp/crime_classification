Home Office figures published yesterday revealed the Metropolitan Police as the fourth worst performing force in England and Wales.London's constabulary is the largest in the UK, but was branded poor at investigating crime, dealing with anti-social behaviour and ensuring equality and equity in dealing with various communities. In a performance league table compiled from reports into all 43 forces in England and Wales, West Mercia was named the best performing, followed by Northumbria and Hampshire. The table is compiled from HM Inspectorate of Constabulary's annual assesments.
