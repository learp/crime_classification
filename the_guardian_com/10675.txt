A man has been shot dead in south London, Scotland Yard said tonight.Officers were called to White Hart Street in Kennington, south London at 9.10pm.They found a man with gunshot wounds. Paramedics and the air ambulance were called but he was pronounced dead at the scene.A member of staff at the White Hart pub in Kennington Lane said customers at the front of the building had heard gun fire.He said: "We saw loads of police cars around and we heard that someone had been shot or was firing a gun."A couple of the guys at the front said they had heard gun shots."The area has been cordoned off while police officers investigate.
